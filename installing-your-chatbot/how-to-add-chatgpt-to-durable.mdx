## Add your AI support agent to your **Durable** website

### In SiteSpeakAI

Log into your SiteSpeakAI account and select your agent from the dropdown menu at the top, click on the **Install Agent** section in the sidebar, select the **Javascript** tab and then click on the **Copy Code** button to copy your agent code snippet.
![uploads/help-center/images/BPzU6Yvm17jEaHl2xgsF2CndIf3aNOKdebYEUuxD.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/BPzU6Yvm17jEaHl2xgsF2CndIf3aNOKdebYEUuxD.png)

### In Durable

1. Log in to your **Durable** website builder dashboard and select **Website**
![1 - Durable - Select Website on dashboard.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/TrJ4OqCbFVi4fyk3DJSDHjfWdCY9b48d0aLCK6cQ.png)

2. Select the **Settings** tab
![2 - Durable - Select the Settings tab.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/PrAgeZkTKluuTtw2Mm30aSD65f6VncMLgdizVGQn.png)

3. Insert your support agent snippet in the **Head Code** text input box under **Custom code** and click on **Save** to save the changes to your website
![3 - Durable - Insert snippet and Save.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/FWgtYZ56mpbMIkks4X9c3Dd24XhCmapks5bZki6c.png)

4. Your AI support agent button will now be displayed on your **Durable** website page
![4 - Durable - chatbot button.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/O5Q8qsyyFaduJ8MWS4Exso7a0QMJffO8maeI7IZc.png)

5. If you click on the button it will display the AI support agent dialogue
![5 - Durable - chatbot dialogue.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/949uC2SObVQ4BMeaC6FOMjRpamlTpWyq76ZcySMI.png)

Your AI support agent has now been added to your **Durable** website and your visitors/customers can start interacting with it!
