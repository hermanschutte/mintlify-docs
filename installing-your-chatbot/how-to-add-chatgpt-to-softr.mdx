You want to embed your custom trained chatbot in your **Softr** generated app. Here's how to add ChatGPT to Softr with SiteSpeakAI.

## Add your chatbot to your **Softr** generated app

<InstallCopyCode />

### In Softr

1. On your **Softr** dashboard, select the app to add your chatbot to
![1 - softr.io - Dashboard - Select App.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/q8mpwru7Na2Cr17kbk4ZWmCmRcqTltvu5iJDmoh4.png)

2. On the page editor, select **Settings**
![2 - softr.io - Select Settings.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/oYUXvzNDbrznj7TuV3iT8Qqj0OU17WGsXDw2wLIv.png)

3. Scroll down on list and select **Custom Code** to add your snippet code
![3 - softr.io - Select Custom Code.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/zTf0UGFTSI5dJ807sm8f7U4qT7IMxCJiNOlsA33H.png)

4. In the **Add Custom Code** text box, insert snippet under **Code inside header** and click on **Save**
![4 - softr.io - Insert snippet.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/imMhW2NS821gnO8H7tUgpLCRYwDbdMKhJZrymBUC.png)

5. Click on **Publish** to publish your changes
![5 - softr.io - Publish.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/kvI4ia66ualwXJhWnDDEfqljKgibx7rNC9YPqk4D.png)

6. Your chatbot button will now be displayed on your app interface
![6 - softr.io - Chatbot button.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/3HEDlglHRkN3vqzUraNBiGlmB3fCVoa1tZtjTkn5.png)

7. If you click on the button it will display the chatbot dialogue
![7 - softr.io - Chatbot dialogue.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/QB8kNSv3NRsboQMLod1CyD6DZclXjRFNWvWitFTT.png)

Your chatbot is now added to your **Softr** app interface and your customers can start interacting with it!
