## Add your chatbot to your Carrd website

<InstallCopyCode />
 
### In Carrd

Go to the **Carrd** page where you want to add your chatbot.
 
1. Click the **+** icon and select **embed**

2. Add your code under **Code** on the left-hand section

3. Save your page and preview your chatbot.
