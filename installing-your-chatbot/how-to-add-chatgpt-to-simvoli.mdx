You want to embed your custom trained chatbot in your **Simvoli** website page. Here's how to add ChatGPT to Simvoli with SiteSpeakAI.

## Add your chatbot to your **Simvoli** website

<InstallCopyCode />

### In Simvoli

1. Log in to your **Simvoli** website builder dashboard and select your website

2. Click on **Add Widget** on the left-side toolbar

![1 - Simvoli - click on Add Widget.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/QlMYZ4TV0yatMeDlOcNB3eRvzu9kiDIMb8BZ7vut.png)

3. Scroll down on the Widget list and select the **CODE** widget under **OTHER**

![2 - Simvoli - select CODE.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/PNtQ7SJXPX25e7erIwBiQw2upcd1bh8JZvlfirRu.png)

4. Drag the **CODE** widget onto your page

![3 - Simvoli - drag the CODE widget onto your page.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/06RUYnlGtx9B0YF0y3haDFW0g2fvjnMOSgsIhutR.png)

5. Insert your snippet in the **Source Code** text input box

![4 - Simvoli - insert your snippet.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/DZDEN3WmWczfyOas5qH1OnWzhBaeikj5zIPael2J.png)

6. Click on **Save** to update your website

![5 - Simvoli - click on Save.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/awYFtW9fZRmhbHq2MoQvQuuCIt5K5JfiEXkfPxb7.png)

7. Your chatbot button will now be displayed on your Simvoli website

![6 - Simvoli - chatbot button on page.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/ZOM6vFj6UnSBfxSD65clAQoqHZQwSxMuGn5XkuKe.png)

8. If you click on the button it will display the chatbot dialogue

![7 - Simvoli - chatbot dialogue on page.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/3Rl2Gfw8oM04zH9iBMU38r0YNobZpWDPo8NhOuH5.png)

Your chatbot is now added to your **Simvoli** website and your customers can start interacting with it!
