You want to embed your custom trained chatbot in your **pixpa** website page. Here's how to add ChatGPT to pixpa with SiteSpeakAI.

## Add your chatbot to your **pixpa** website

### In SiteSpeakAI

Select your chatbot from the dropdown menu at the top, click on the **Install Chatbot** section in the sidebar and then click on the **Copy Code** button to copy your code snippet.

![Copy JS code](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/NR3B7H65sLTjC214hXbfEkUxkykTLIMwEEm5gmFL.png)

### In pixpa

1. Log into your **pixpa** account and on the dashboard select **Edit site design** for the site you want to add your chatbot to

![Select website on dashboard](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/MPkOePCmC6IpfXbDUUCxQyvsS6an5Eul06z2bBDr.png)

2. Click on the exit button to exit Design mode

![Exit Design mode](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/yuNkeOXROpUsrmiL0Pd0Qvq8ISY4yOldz8RvPowk.png)

3. Select the **Website** option on the left sidebar list

![Select Website](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/3tqxzCl2LFTHt4LLAAqFGxaQc46XzOmjJUogoCfv.png)

4. On the Website edit page, select **Edit content** to allow editing the content of the website page

![Select Edit content](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/8Enkr8d9LYTuCh5BOldZZFU9QM0MP8SdcfSY8Opd.png)

5. On the **Edit content** page, select **Settings**

![Select Settings](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/xsvoChPwPhPsjxVu94vz4AUyc5qBuBBQKjSDum3e.png)

6. Scroll down on the **Page Settings** dialogue to the **Page Code Injection** option, insert your chatbot snippet in the Page Code Injection input text box and click on **Save**

![Insert snippet](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/qp5RmoAk6CsWVR3h2r1c2SssUFYjwSRtqrOwJDIJ.png)

7. Your chatbot button will now be displayed on your **pixpa** website

![Chatbot button](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/DLuQnGUrH6luLapEnpgqxTx38qswgFAWhl3FB8g3.png)

8. If you click on the button it will display your chatbot dialogue

![Chatbot dialogue](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/xOP7b4tBpwBUYGQwV5NriU9b9Pu2ip3EiVTYzj99.png)

Your chatbot has now been added to your **pixpa** website and your customers/visitors can start interacting with it!
