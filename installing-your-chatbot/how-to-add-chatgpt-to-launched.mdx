You want to embed your custom trained AI support agent in your **LAUNCHED** website page. Here's how to add an AI support agent to LAUNCHED with SiteSpeakAI.

**LAUNCHED** is a super-easy website builder designed for *non-techies*. It's built around a unique "block" system, you drag and drop pre-designed blocks (like text, images, videos, forms, buttons, etc.) onto a page to build your site.  The biggest selling point is its speed and simplicity. You don't need to know HTML, CSS, or any coding. It focuses on creating beautiful, functional websites quickly, especially for portfolios, landing pages, small business sites, and online stores. It also automatically handles SEO (search engine optimization) to help your site get found on Google.

**Website URL:** [https://launched.site/](https://launched.site/)

## Add your AI support agent to your **LAUNCHED** website

### In SiteSpeakAI

Log into your SiteSpeakAI account and select your agent from the dropdown menu at the top, click on the **Install Agent** section in the sidebar, select the **Javascript** tab and then click on the **Copy Code** button to copy your agent code snippet.
![uploads/help-center/images/I7xS1tbeukY87eG90bOBnQDGXnQpJQx0n3bCVvzd.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/I7xS1tbeukY87eG90bOBnQDGXnQpJQx0n3bCVvzd.png)

### In LAUNCHED

1. Login to **LAUNCHED** and on your dashboard, select **EDIT** for the first content section of your page (in this case it is **Home**)
![uploads/help-center/images/qA2jb0qlgzR5WcyKrra6j8uaDzdnf1KQCHcoZqwD.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/qA2jb0qlgzR5WcyKrra6j8uaDzdnf1KQCHcoZqwD.png)

2. Scroll down on the page editor screen & select **\<>**  under **Content** to enable the **HTML** editor
![uploads/help-center/images/CRrNP21mdk6IMa63MWp6rblZBJ8wurSRHfMaS4Cd.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/CRrNP21mdk6IMa63MWp6rblZBJ8wurSRHfMaS4Cd.png)

3. Insert a line above any existing content in the **Content** text box and insert your snippet
![uploads/help-center/images/6zCiH7bFXbthh304I65YnydV8Q9I7BJZM7JeiU2v.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/6zCiH7bFXbthh304I65YnydV8Q9I7BJZM7JeiU2v.png)

![uploads/help-center/images/QaUqJWPenH3yyiWGAwuegDYXCmpw2CV7l8wHd3xg.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/QaUqJWPenH3yyiWGAwuegDYXCmpw2CV7l8wHd3xg.png)

4. Scroll down and click on **SAVED** to save & publish your changes
![uploads/help-center/images/4Nf2hlO1E4E0OsztKWL7g3hOeGz6yVWgEJ1ebqjI.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/4Nf2hlO1E4E0OsztKWL7g3hOeGz6yVWgEJ1ebqjI.png)

5. Your AI support agent button will now be displayed on your **LAUNCHED** website page
![uploads/help-center/images/vHeUTt48CoKnr3hKySupdwemQbVLnsrY5S64kCVa.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/vHeUTt48CoKnr3hKySupdwemQbVLnsrY5S64kCVa.png)

6. If you click on the button it will display the AI support agent dialogue
![uploads/help-center/images/pBhemI1zqrkrZfU3lWJtIOHbVfhrGN525ek8HujB.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/pBhemI1zqrkrZfU3lWJtIOHbVfhrGN525ek8HujB.png)

Your AI support agent has now been added to your **LAUNCHED** website and your visitors/customers can start interacting with it!
