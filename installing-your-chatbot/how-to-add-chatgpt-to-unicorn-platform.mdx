You want to embed your custom trained AI support agent in your **Unicorn Platform** website page. Here's how to add an AI support agent to Unicorn Platform with SiteSpeakAI.

## Add your AI support agent to your **Unicorn Platform** website

**Unicorn Platform** is an AI-powered, no-code website and landing page builder designed for startups, SaaS products, mobile apps, blogs, directories, and personal pages. It enables busy founders and solo entrepreneurs to create professional, responsive sites in minutes without design or development skills.

**Website:** [**Unicorn Platform**](https://unicornplatform.com/).

### In SiteSpeakAI

Log into your **SiteSpeakAI** account and select your agent from the dropdown menu at the top, click on the **Install Agent** section in the sidebar, select the **Javascript** tab and then click on the **Copy Code** button to copy your agent code snippet.
![Copy code snippet](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/fj1UKyuf7JXRapwTqXi194g75IbfrWgw2Y1JWAJ3.png)

### In Unicorn Platform

1. Log into your **Unicorn** account and on the dashboard, select your website from the dropdown if it is not already selected
![Select website](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/B2oco8odjyQpyNBfIkq900PmakVmaqHTDxa0i3dr.png)

2. Click on **Settings**
![Click Settings](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/vFbjKPuGdD8lNThsCyaU9cePUFh3jsl1QliJMzFY.png)

3. Under **General** settings, scroll down to **Global \<head\> HTML code**, insert your snippet and click on the **Save** icon to save your changes
![Insert snippet](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/46VukTSifM8bgNUlzf9h5RGEZflXZZh9o3udGcKk.png)

4. Your chatbot button will now be displayed on your **Unicorn Platform** website page
![Chatbot button](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/HYrHUXWgRSg7FGOFdVBubDOMRtniRt6vK5Rsh28j.png)

5. If you click on the button it will display the chatbot dialogue
![Chatbot dialogue](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/xA4sK3QCycnfnJTG75IFJHw35jW13CGCa9uazZv9.png)

Your AI support agent has now been added to your **Unicorn Platform** website and your visitors/customers can start interacting with it!
