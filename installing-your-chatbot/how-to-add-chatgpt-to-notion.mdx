You want to embed your custom trained chatbot in your Notion website. Here's how to add ChatGPT to Notion with SiteSpeakAI.

With Notion, you will need to use the **iFrame** option to embed your chatbot.

## Add your chatbot to your Notion website

### In SiteSpeakAI

Select your chatbot from the dropdown menu at the top, click on the **Install Chatbot** section in the sidebar, then click on the **iFrame** tab and then on the **Copy Code** button to copy your **iFrame** code snippet.
 
![copy-code-snippet.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/bjMJzOTDAEBcxF1yBZ6VpIpWfwY1wMDWSRBZR8jv.png)
 
### In Notion

1. Go to the Notion page where you would like to add your chatbot.

2. Type **/embed**

3. In the modal, paste the **iFrame** code snippet that you just copied.
