You want to embed your custom trained chatbot in your **upsitely** website page. Here's how to add ChatGPT to upsitely with SiteSpeakAI.

## Add your chatbot to your **upsitely** website

<InstallCopyCode />

### In upsitely

1. Log in to your **upsitely** website builder dashboard and select your website

![1 - upsitely - select your website.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/3C6OaIrHRYhjxk9lRvrarQITZJrDaBHEaL0CnlmD.png)

2. Click on **Menu icon** on the top hamburger menu

![2 - upsitely - click on Menu icon.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/TY7vfOwlkt3Na5AECe23GAjyDaEFVo6CeZFyyuNH.png)

3. Select **Head Code**

![3 - upsitely - select Head Code.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/oJeLcrhPXWtuXsrACLsQSXSB5MbYrnoLEDKWX4SW.png)

4. Insert your snippet under **Site header code** and click on **Save**

![4 - upsitely - insert snippet.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/CLcv2CXyXECK991wGjoTmFKyd1BVrK1EIPYk8wS2.png)

5. Click on **Publish**

![5 - upsitely - click on Publish.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/Ww0bL0RF41dtLBDYFuIt44nQyWsv50AurCWQv4tZ.png)

6. Your chatbot button will now be displayed on your **upsitely** website

![6 - upsitely - chatbot button.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/xr3PcwOwxLGJEFMfldYBL39gFKQ5nv6ktSAh3SSp.png)

7. If you click on the button it will display the chatbot dialogue

![7 - upsitely - chatbot dialogue.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/vqbHgZFgQ3BV9wmWjMETWzzvROcOVdNLqnAOPywP.png)

Your chatbot is now added to your **upsitely** website and your customers can start interacting with it!
