You want to embed your custom trained chatbot in your **Zegashop** website page. Here's how to add ChatGPT to Zegashop with SiteSpeakAI.

## Add your chatbot to your **Zegashop** website

### In SiteSpeakAI

Select your chatbot from the dropdown menu at the top, click on the **Install Chatbot** section in the sidebar and then click on the **Copy Code** button to copy your code snippet.
![0 - Zegashop - Copy js code.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/JQlzitNqH8amRU9wLbrEdbtShw0YBNVPt3BMGHR3.png)

### In Zegashop

1. Log into your **Zegashop** account and on the dashboard, select **Configure**
![1 - Zegashop - Select Configure on dashboard.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/FoFDju4Vz7tYgZBN5yqCYdY7LKjLIZalhUgztHXj.png)

2. Select **General** under Configure
![2 - Zegashop - Select General under Configure.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/gfyfZ6JFWXDQCzvUcER7mx4Uhiggti6sfbJm8kKr.png)

3. Select the **Content** tab, insert your chatbot snippet in the **Custom Javascript** Scripts box and click on **Save**
![3 - Zegashop - Insert your snippet and click on Save.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/nT21mjsdphpiCU7IehoF03RaBsh03UpE3IGCgIOP.png)

4. Voila! Your chatbot button will now be displayed on your **Zegashop** website
![4 - Zegashop - Voila! Your chatbot button on your webpage .png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/8hFaFgU8KPqW7fwSY0n3bHJAGW7o2iYK7eW01f4P.png)

5. If you click on the button it will display the chatbot dialogue
![5 - Zegashop - Click on it and your chatbot dialogue displays on your webpage .png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/HCCW4MmY4DQqJs4GU87WTpVQwaXU8OccPfXIGka4.png)

Your chatbot is now added to your **Zegashop** website and your customers/visitors can start interacting with it!
