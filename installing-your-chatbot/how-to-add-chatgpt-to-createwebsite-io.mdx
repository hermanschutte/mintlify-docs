You want to embed your custom trained AI support agent in your **createwebsite.io** website page. Here's how to add an AI support agent to createwebsite.io with SiteSpeakAI.

**createwebsite.io** is a drag-and-drop website builder designed for beginners. It's incredibly easy to use â€“ you don't need any coding knowledge. You simply choose a template, customize it with pre-built blocks (like text, images, buttons, and forms), and then publish your website. They offer a wide variety of templates for different types of sites (business, portfolio, blog, etc.) and have a free plan with limited features. It's a good option if you want a simple, professional-looking website quickly and without technical hassle.

**Website:** [**createwebsite.io** ](https://createwebsite.io/)

## Add your AI support agent to your **createwebsite.io** website

### In SiteSpeakAI

Log into your SiteSpeakAI account and select your agent from the dropdown menu at the top, click on the **Install Agent** section in the sidebar, select the **Javascript** tab and then copy the URL of the Javascript (.js) file (**DO NOT** click on **Copy Code**!!). You will use this later when we insert your snippet.
![uploads/help-center/images/pDlVg7EiE7D3hQ2cItIBvCuBnwVnINZyqDymzw1j.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/pDlVg7EiE7D3hQ2cItIBvCuBnwVnINZyqDymzw1j.png)

### In createwebsite.io

1. Log into your **createwebsite.io** account and on your dashboard select the project to edit

![uploads/help-center/images/yxZ0qwm6ChlsYItmTqSl38675OiOtBxoWWF2d9C5.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/yxZ0qwm6ChlsYItmTqSl38675OiOtBxoWWF2d9C5.png)

2. Click on **Settings**

![uploads/help-center/images/MFnENotyXx1rw4bRpzQGsEtglQJSs0g4kJsJhCyE.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/MFnENotyXx1rw4bRpzQGsEtglQJSs0g4kJsJhCyE.png)

3. Under **Project Settings** scroll down and select **+ Add head tag**

![uploads/help-center/images/VgIHSKg4AskWfAIQWLwYmXiLINts4UTfOi2zs4Nm.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/VgIHSKg4AskWfAIQWLwYmXiLINts4UTfOi2zs4Nm.png)

4. Insert URL of .js file that you copied at the start and click on **Save Changes**

![uploads/help-center/images/VBT8ddgewNAkwpQMtoqjibAqtxV5JEIAEoyVjtQ3.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/VBT8ddgewNAkwpQMtoqjibAqtxV5JEIAEoyVjtQ3.png)

5. Click on **X** to close the **Project Settings**

![uploads/help-center/images/jrEw9Vp9z1BOekev6U9tKUEqbJLLGka1sugBq9e0.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/jrEw9Vp9z1BOekev6U9tKUEqbJLLGka1sugBq9e0.png)

6. Click on **Sync changes** to update your website

![uploads/help-center/images/TluHUmTGpitmwVs2LQVUJxMmpL49EfRbIdjZuoO1.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/TluHUmTGpitmwVs2LQVUJxMmpL49EfRbIdjZuoO1.png)

7. Your AI support agent button will now be displayed on all your **createwebsite.io** website pages

![uploads/help-center/images/iumxX0kMc1AU6neSoGdVPb9wxu5QdYgrdiyUtB0Y.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/iumxX0kMc1AU6neSoGdVPb9wxu5QdYgrdiyUtB0Y.png)

![uploads/help-center/images/sdBjq6prR1HqwUplqsuPx7xtYR9XJKCakNdNdrNs.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/sdBjq6prR1HqwUplqsuPx7xtYR9XJKCakNdNdrNs.png)

![uploads/help-center/images/MDWAMB5YYCWXSTKd1F46PLktC8Syd41VjAVHeE8H.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/MDWAMB5YYCWXSTKd1F46PLktC8Syd41VjAVHeE8H.png)

8. If you click on the button it will display the AI support agent dialogue

![uploads/help-center/images/s6Dc9ZaUcZP80Ot62xacQATGzt4vus6yMcXlaTUq.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/s6Dc9ZaUcZP80Ot62xacQATGzt4vus6yMcXlaTUq.png)

Your AI support agent has now been added to your **createwebsite.io** website and your visitors/customers can start interacting with it!
