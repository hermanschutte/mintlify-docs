You want to embed your custom trained chatbot in your **Silex** website page. Here's how to add ChatGPT to Silex with SiteSpeakAI.

## Add your chatbot to your **Silex** website

### In SiteSpeakAI

Select your chatbot from the dropdown menu at the top, click on the **Install Chatbot** section in the sidebar and then click on the **Copy Code** button to copy your code snippet.
![0 - Silex - Copy JS Code.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/scKdU3lQPAU1j6CSH8JzJxuYtBBYwfnCoealSraA.png)

### In Silex

1. Log into your **Silex** account and open the website you want to add your chatbot to

2. Select the **Code** item on the left sidebar
![1 - Silex - Select Code item.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/7weXMRvpC3RHGbBN6ZueAt2YSW7jhL6fGk2GOqV1.png)

3. Select **HTML HEAD editor** on the Code Menu
![2 - Silex - Select HTML HEAD editor on the Code Menu.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/JOMYEzHKRUpivaIT20btW7rS1dy4cbgoXym7DFYW.png)

4.  Insert your snippet in the **HTML editor** and click on **X** to close the editor
![3 - Silex - Insert your snippet in the  HTML editor and click on X to close editor.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/84ENXWYZePG5ut60fNLzx7X9ZO1wtWA0PLQ8fvmP.png)

5. Click on the **File menu** icon to open the File dialogue to save your website
![4 - Silex - Click on the File menu icon to open the File dialogue .png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/eQ5YoFvlAsY7J4Yeted91NC0RYYp80pmr5UNcCIB.png)

6. Click on **Save website** to save the changes you made to your website
![5 - Silex - Click on Save website .png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/nkvdhAY1crLskyPDCOIuRQsuuahl76KPvTfonGxw.png)

7. Your chatbot button will now be displayed on your website page
![6 - Silex - Chatbot button on website .png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/PcpOCygGxu3oyYYppTgJswSnmLis1MKjhqP35vgy.png)

8. If you click on the button it will display the chatbot dialogue
![7 - Silex - Chatbot dialogue .png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/V6eQQmSO6lUuvl0LrO70gxOqBOGL5c1e0wQQcMoX.png)

That's it! You have successfully added your SiteSpeakAI no-code chatbot to your **Silex** website. Your customers/visitors can now start interacting with it, asking all the questions they have.
