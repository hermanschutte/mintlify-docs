You want to embed your custom trained chatbot in your **Publii** website page. Here's how to add ChatGPT to Publii with SiteSpeakAI.

## Add your chatbot to your **Publii** website

**Please note**:

Publii is not an on-line (web based) website builder. It is installed on your computer as an application.

The default `server` setup is on your local computer but it can be setup to use **FTP** or **SFTP** to transfer the code to a host of your choice.

<InstallCopyCode />

### In Publii

1.	Start the **Publii** application and (if you have more than one site created on Publii) select the website from the dropdown list

![1 - Publii - Dashboard.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/LgqtRAoX4eU27HbFWvfpSxjokHcbAtpryyhABQuG.png)
...
![1b - Publii - Select website.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/vTE2wdJ5rKtXO775TZwOPVJW0affBw6QTjadzSeW.png)

2.	Select **Tools & Plugins**

![2 - Publii - Select Tools & Plugins.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/R5j5UCZTs24xtsOZBFJWBZbQ0NBww4XjsqN6olka.png)

3.	Select **Custom HTML**

![3 - Publii - Select Custom HTML.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/0avFVhGns4JA4Zy9jdrFe2AcObLRqXq5bBbeqM21.png)

4.	Insert your snippet in the **Head** (or **Footer**) part of your website and click on **Save & Preview**

![4 - Publii - Insert snippet in Head part.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/Vm6WOu5rt2XR0017Zkb6gPkKiGy1LiCMjf89ZH7H.png)

5.	Your chatbot button will now be displayed on your Publii website

![5 - Publii - Chatbot button.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/Qq9Dkpcl5ZHF4Yx5BFDsB6ls5WmJdLy6npGca4a0.png)

6.	If you click on the button it will display the chatbot dialogue

![6 - Publii - Chatbot dialogue.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/BmahMfPWh8pDTxCOVHKEvjel6DGuZEYd7O4NGQ8L.png)

Your chatbot is now added to your **Publii** website and your customers can start interacting with it!
