You want to embed your custom trained chatbot in your ZOHO website. Here's how to add ChatGPT to ZOHO with SiteSpeakAI.

## Add your chatbot to your Squarespace website

<InstallCopyCode />

### In ZOHO

On your dashboard select the website to edit under **My Sites**

![ZOHO - 1. Dashboard select website to edit.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/FVBUWUfr4fJtK1Gb2qypHZfmS4NtqJBkPeIAwrNy.png)

1. Select **Settings** in the website editor

![ZOHO - 2. Select Settings on Website editor.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/itxFOUT9vQj3eXqpM4SmaIaZhVStBBQAUhTNcAhM.png)

2. Under **GENERAL** select **Header and Footer Code**

![ZOHO - 3. Select Headr and Footer Code.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/hNCLCX23a7XXZvk2NaFAKEUgUS0ASEdKWpaRyLMn.png)

3. Insert your snippet in the **Header Code** part under **General Settings** and click on **Save** to save your code

![ZOHO - 4. Insert snippet under Header Code.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/Yi5UV2LXgNxyYxVHrGrJrWkVjBifdqhe8PV8BlFa.png)

4. Click on **Back to Builder** to return to the website editor

![ZOHO - 5. Back to Builder.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/iKSxzqgxF1D0HedfBCk69I1APuVzEiOY04rnHgJA.png)

5. Select **Publish** to publish your website to view your chatbot. ***Note!*** **Preview** does not display your chatbot.

![ZOHO - 6. Select Publish to publish website.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/ZKJ8zdi5QC7x51rwhSdjV0XvY62djNYU5PPvrxF0.png)

6. Your chatbot button will now be displayed on your website page

![ZOHO - 7. Chatbot button on website.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/azbAdgzkUTNFm6ugjgV2BJCkPNX63nXgcNmXFJZW.png)

7. If you click on the button it will display the chatbot dialogue

![ZOHO - 8. Chatbot dialogue on website.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/ue1VwbSxZ6DimmiSeC7BVRiRMdOKLz4srWK8iQLu.png)

Your chatbot is now added to your ZOHO website and your customers can start interacting with it.
