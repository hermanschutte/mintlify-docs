You want to embed your custom trained AI support agent in your **MotoCMS** website page. Here's how to add an AI support agent to MotoCMS with SiteSpeakAI.

**MotoCMS** is a website builder that's a bit different from the usual drag-and-drop options like Wix or Squarespace. It's designed for more complex websites, think online stores, portfolios, and sites with lots of interactive elements. It combines a visual editor with a powerful backend meaning you can easily build the *front-end* (what visitors see) with a simple interface, but then you can also manage the *back-end*, things like databases, SEO, and integrations, using a more technical panel. 

**Website URL:** [https://www.motocms.com/](https://www.motocms.com/)

## Add your AI support agent to your **MotoCMS** website

### In SiteSpeakAI

Log into your SiteSpeakAI account and select your agent from the dropdown menu at the top, click on the **Install Agent** section in the sidebar, select the **Javascript** tab and then click on the **Copy Code** button to copy your agent code snippet.
![uploads/help-center/images/tdZalI6KArTsn5mIo3yBa6bI6YaB1kLNJPBYKazC.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/tdZalI6KArTsn5mIo3yBa6bI6YaB1kLNJPBYKazC.png)

### In MotoCMS

1. Log in to your **MotoCMS** website builder dashboard and select **Edit Pages**
![uploads/help-center/images/pKVMOVvW7feaC3Xjc7jHFMvtCL44xvxcELMUMmLz.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/pKVMOVvW7feaC3Xjc7jHFMvtCL44xvxcELMUMmLz.png)

2. Click on **Settings**
![uploads/help-center/images/6EOCOxMR4eRWwy2IuDTRo8NPhanwwM77Hn3bkOmu.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/6EOCOxMR4eRWwy2IuDTRo8NPhanwwM77Hn3bkOmu.png)

3. Select **CODE INJECTION**
![uploads/help-center/images/LNTWHDlKn52RtTRQ9ZyagfY8rch6Pogr00xOXAye.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/LNTWHDlKn52RtTRQ9ZyagfY8rch6Pogr00xOXAye.png)

4. Insert your snippet code under **Header** and click on **Save Changes**
![uploads/help-center/images/NK3TpbR44KTLNzpZGo4KhC2Dfb5S2KcpEzqQYJK8.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/NK3TpbR44KTLNzpZGo4KhC2Dfb5S2KcpEzqQYJK8.png)

5. Click on **View Site**  to view your page
![uploads/help-center/images/RASvj4PguNeSCYCEWkeqNiwkPRuFY8q0f4kze2Sy.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/RASvj4PguNeSCYCEWkeqNiwkPRuFY8q0f4kze2Sy.png)

6. Your AI support agent button will now be displayed on your **MotoCMS** website page
![uploads/help-center/images/cBFlDQHRNrwWgWRNx6K6Y7Kn47yWiU9CwJsROE9r.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/cBFlDQHRNrwWgWRNx6K6Y7Kn47yWiU9CwJsROE9r.png)

7. If you click on the button it will display the AI support agent dialogue
![uploads/help-center/images/NglpUvB3JoObY91RsUAWoqEodgQmWt2mSgeFUZEt.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/NglpUvB3JoObY91RsUAWoqEodgQmWt2mSgeFUZEt.png)

Your AI support agent has now been added to your **MotoCMS** website and your visitors/customers can start interacting with it!
