You want to embed your custom trained AI support agent in your **SnapPages** website page. Here's how to add an AI support agent to SnapPages with SiteSpeakAI.

**SnapPages** is a website builder that's known for being *really* easy to use, especially for beginners. It's a drag-and-drop platform, meaning you build your website by simply dragging elements (text boxes, images, buttons, etc.) onto a page and arranging them how you like. It automatically handles the technical stuff like hosting, security, and updates â€“ so you don't need to be a coder.  It's built on top of WordPress, but you don't need to worry about managing WordPress directly.

**Website URL:** [https://snappages.com/](https://snappages.com/)

## Add your AI support agent to your **SnapPages** website

### In SiteSpeakAI

Log into your SiteSpeakAI account and select your agent from the dropdown menu at the top, click on the **Install Agent** section in the sidebar, select the **Javascript** tab and then click on the **Copy Code** button to copy your agent code snippet.
![uploads/help-center/images/Wk5r8l07h1K5awIwIXmEctqBXqvG3B8RF7YkvRjf.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/Wk5r8l07h1K5awIwIXmEctqBXqvG3B8RF7YkvRjf.png)

### In SnapPages

1. Log into your **SnapPages** account and on the dashboard select **Settings** on the left sidebar menu
![uploads/help-center/images/iRATLYynIMLcsmhk0wEpzZwhtP7YDrIjcBhMIFyu.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/iRATLYynIMLcsmhk0wEpzZwhtP7YDrIjcBhMIFyu.png)

2. Select **Code**, insert your snippet under **Header Code** and click on **Save Settings**
![uploads/help-center/images/tHcVyDXW7lRRz8kgIMLSN9py6H6gxPRCv3rJSNAa.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/tHcVyDXW7lRRz8kgIMLSN9py6H6gxPRCv3rJSNAa.png)

3. Your AI support agent button will now be displayed on your **SnapPages** website page
![uploads/help-center/images/ZJqZjCR3j7BX8YK7dZ0EfbHQSO0hZ1iMvZn9BO1R.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/ZJqZjCR3j7BX8YK7dZ0EfbHQSO0hZ1iMvZn9BO1R.png)

4. If you click on the button it will display the AI support agent dialogue
![uploads/help-center/images/LRGP6qfysTZ6QDdk208LBdtI8V1Ro3WWJvQv0lG9.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/LRGP6qfysTZ6QDdk208LBdtI8V1Ro3WWJvQv0lG9.png)

Your AI support agent has now been added to your **SnapPages** website and your visitors/customers can start interacting with it!
