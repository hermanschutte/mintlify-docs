You want to embed your custom trained chatbot in your **Umso** website page. Here's how to add ChatGPT to Umso with SiteSpeakAI.

## Add your chatbot to your **Umso** website

<InstallCopyCode />

### In Umso

1. Log in to your **Umso** website builder dashboard and select the website to edit under **sites**

![1 - Umso - select site to Edit.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/mT2ykIslbJtUQyCDU2t2Pwy7g01kUUhGmT4UZrkH.png)

2. Click on **Edit Site** to start the site editor

![2 - Umso - click on Edit Site.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/4dGVR7qqALzB0ucewtp3vXQFzi7IgQEJnr33g6QE.png)

3. Under **Content**, select **Site and Page Settings**

![3 - Umso - select site & page settings under content.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/SlJukjVNe7jRh1tiAKG2IcjhE5CORTuWikwWfTpw.png)

4. Under **Site Settings**, select **Custom Site Code**

![4 - Umso - select custom site code under site settings.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/Q6TVCEZC5URAz4socFXXuGCPPO2JptVS0DGYsEUI.png)

5. Insert your snippet code under **Header Code**

![5 - Umso - insert snippet.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/fbYB7pOkFmxx7Pn01XggDH23Kzetl1H7sLaMc2UT.png)

6. Click on **Publish** to publish your page edits to your website

![6 - Umso - click on Publish.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/5AGQiif4ShuMm8CImt5ITKIileomO1w0eJzOtyVS.png)

7. Your chatbot button will now be displayed on your Umso website

![7 - Umso - Chatbot button on Page.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/twJKzl90MLR9KOSI5NxtADqLf8FNFKRBT1aMqGWW.png)

8. If you click on the button it will display the chatbot dialogue

![8 - Umso - Chatbot dialogue on Page.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/PkhzPWwSlz8JmgpPBGr8bRux0fV179rkV6tmrTwz.png)

Your chatbot is now added to your **Umso** website and your customers can start interacting with it!
