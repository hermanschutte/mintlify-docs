You want to embed your custom trained chatbot in your **SimpleShop** website page. Here's how to add ChatGPT to SimpleShop with SiteSpeakAI.

## Add your chatbot to your **SimpleShop** website

### In SiteSpeakAI

Select your chatbot from the dropdown menu at the top, click on the **Install Chatbot** section in the sidebar and then click on the **Copy Code** button to copy your code snippet.

![Copy JS code](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/ggjOIhcbnBMPoezBFr3b3JPuAlpUR4SiDGQNWqJv.png)

### In SimpleShop

1. Log into your **SimpleShop** account and on your Dashboard select **All Pages** under **Content Pages** on the left side menu

![Select All Pages](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/g3D229x3sgHyAbjcQ0DQPJn2o1O1wDIev2kQPVk3.png)

2. Select **Edit** for the page you want to add your chatbot button to

![Select Edit](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/jOQCJmTi6EUkI9o6lM3KxbV5aoLW7LINfySMxCUo.png)

3. On the **Edit Page** screen, select **Show Advanced Settings**

![Click on Show Advanced Settings](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/0FaOLJz7zVW8fuKsoIOzep1Lq313QvtavtCB0Vjh.png)

4. On the Advanced Settings list, scroll down to **Header Code**, insert your chatbot snippet in the code box and click on **Save Page**

![Insert snippet](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/fr2vBxQTA7dCXxHBUclhgNEQGdNG8oSE7nxHptaO.png)

5. Your chatbot button will now be displayed on your **SimpleShop** website page

![Chatbot button on page](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/IdnMVZe6znTEPmruYEnHn4kU5EkCvthmEZA0YhK1.png)

6. If you click on the button it will display the chatbot dialogue

![Chatbot dialogue on page](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/3pKKAIZx3qy90xO6YV05WHGyfoFG4uGhJoHc7mUO.png)

Your chatbot is now added to your **SimpleShop** website and your visitors/customers can start interacting with it!
