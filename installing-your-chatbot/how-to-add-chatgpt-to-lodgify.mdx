You want to embed your custom trained AI support agent in your **LODGIFY** website page. Here's how to add an AI support agent to LODGIFY with SiteSpeakAI.

**Lodgify** is a simple and affordable website builder specifically designed for vacation rental owners and property managers. It's built to be incredibly easy to use, even if you have *no* technical skills.  It focuses on helping you quickly create a beautiful, mobile-friendly website to showcase your rental property, manage bookings, and communicate with guests.  It handles things like online booking, payment processing, and guest messaging all in one place.  It's a good option if you want a straightforward, no-fuss way to get your rental online.

**Website URL:** [https://www.lodgify.com/](https://www.lodgify.com/)

## Add your AI support agent to your **LODGIFY** website

### In SiteSpeakAI

Log into your SiteSpeakAI account and select your agent from the dropdown menu at the top, click on the **Install Agent** section in the sidebar, select the **Javascript** tab and then click on the **Copy Code** button to copy your agent code snippet.
![uploads/help-center/images/c8UvSoj0QAjdAQ4OnfZwQFCZhfUQikkdafBzNXGQ.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/c8UvSoj0QAjdAQ4OnfZwQFCZhfUQikkdafBzNXGQ.png)

### In LODGIFY

1. Log into your **LODGIFY** account and select **Website builder** on the Dashboard
![uploads/help-center/images/xYSYx8ky9y6bCw3oVXSmMqIskobDAqQXfd5kvShR.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/xYSYx8ky9y6bCw3oVXSmMqIskobDAqQXfd5kvShR.png)

2. Click on the **Settings** tab and select **Advanced**
![uploads/help-center/images/iH9qpDThL7lvlRYXlcmWV4rJUUb6PWEfmKzvB7ie.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/iH9qpDThL7lvlRYXlcmWV4rJUUb6PWEfmKzvB7ie.png)

3. Insert your snippet in the **Footer - Custom Javascript** text box under **Javascript Injection**
![uploads/help-center/images/K3UZn1IOcrui1W2gtHOURhVtKdrr98ssPmJCjx5n.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/K3UZn1IOcrui1W2gtHOURhVtKdrr98ssPmJCjx5n.png)

4. Click on **Save** to save your contents and then click on **Publish website** to publish your changes to your live website
![uploads/help-center/images/iVFOUDSjRE1QreilVSTBG8ka7Z0GPaVgFdKPI66X.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/iVFOUDSjRE1QreilVSTBG8ka7Z0GPaVgFdKPI66X.png)

5. Your AI support agent button will now be displayed on your **LODGIFY** website page
![uploads/help-center/images/aRAiyZGC5hbnoDRgSW5HCnbVPBng5E4xJ1C8H7SK.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/aRAiyZGC5hbnoDRgSW5HCnbVPBng5E4xJ1C8H7SK.png)

6. If you click on the button it will display the AI support agent dialogue
![uploads/help-center/images/6bVIZMmHLvJWJmGBKib8CZKaCzAjT0sgOmGzjJBQ.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/6bVIZMmHLvJWJmGBKib8CZKaCzAjT0sgOmGzjJBQ.png)

Your AI support agent has now been added to your **LODGIFY** website and your visitors/customers can start interacting with it!
