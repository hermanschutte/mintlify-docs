If you want to add your custom trained chatbot to your Slack workspace, here are the steps you need to take.

## Add your chatbot to your Slack workspace

### In SiteSpeakAI

Select your chatbot from the dropdown menu at the top, click on the **Install Chatbot** section in the sidebar, then click on the **Slack** button.

![Untitled.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/JOWC5mSzeqK05QvuT0xJGi29Ox8kXLvOm7aCvJmo.png)

Click on the **Connect to Slack** button.

![Untitled.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/rCTcHDHBUOcVnypf0Talg3HNeHkoC6OxjKNtzRG1.png)

Select the workspace you would like to add your bot to in the right corner, and the click on the **Allow** button to grant SiteSpeakAI permission to your workspace.

![Untitled.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/tW5c9dpr3DmjcKViR21eHqPNANRJcLzSbRawnxk3.png)

If access was granted, you'll see a message about how to invite the bot to a channel in your workspace.

### In Slack

Select the channel you would like to add your chatbot to.

![Untitled.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/LOn7JjizFPXCFHgRdPny6dD0jOjN0DvtcSLYRxyH.png)

Then invite the chatbot by mentioning it: ***@SiteSpeakAI***

![Untitled.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/EFuBLHEjMSUFFgdSkPpMPmaRb4AWS760mUH2prY7.png)

Click the **Add to Channel** button.

Your chatbot is now added and you and your team can now begin asking it questions.

![Untitled.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/WHzZ6sXxNb0d8is47URKlnvVFT43DGBXbehRfYBy.png)
