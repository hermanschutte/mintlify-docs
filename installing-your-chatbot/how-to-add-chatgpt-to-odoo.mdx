You want to embed your custom trained AI support agent in your **odoo** website page. Here's how to add an AI support agent to odoo with SiteSpeakAI.

odoo's Website Builder is a surprisingly powerful and easy-to-use tool that lets you create professional-looking websites and landing pages *without* needing to be a coder or designer. It's part of the larger **odoo** suite of business applications, think of it as a way to manage your online presence alongside your business operations. It's drag-and-drop simple. You can visually build your website by adding blocks for text, images, videos, forms, buttons, and more.  It's particularly strong for showcasing products, services, and collecting leads.

**Website URL:** [https://www.odoo.com/website-builder](https://www.odoo.com/website-builder)

## Add your AI support agent to your **odoo** website

### In SiteSpeakAI

Log into your SiteSpeakAI account and select your agent from the dropdown menu at the top, click on the **Install Agent** section in the sidebar, select the **Javascript** tab and then click on the **Copy Code** button to copy your agent code snippet.
![uploads/help-center/images/bRYkWw19eNUF4U21ufp4mc6FHhiP8ke6oq0WKNyr.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/bRYkWw19eNUF4U21ufp4mc6FHhiP8ke6oq0WKNyr.png)

### In odoo

1. Log into your **odoo** account and on the dashboard select **Website**
![uploads/help-center/images/3uz907P13QqhDe0na8Yce54oIyEN5YJQwxw719Ut.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/3uz907P13QqhDe0na8Yce54oIyEN5YJQwxw719Ut.png)

2. On the website page editor select **Edit**
![uploads/help-center/images/RGfcPYJgy76WfmwM2MlIQgy509EXbKz13Jmsw7W6.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/RGfcPYJgy76WfmwM2MlIQgy509EXbKz13Jmsw7W6.png)

3. Click on the **THEME** tab in the editor menu
![uploads/help-center/images/T3FKixgfr7A9rIwB7NCt0iohCVStrzlf3H45DRwW.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/T3FKixgfr7A9rIwB7NCt0iohCVStrzlf3H45DRwW.png)

4. Scroll down on the THEME list and select *\<head> and \</body>* under **Code Injection**
![uploads/help-center/images/5Zq1bExwkBIegFx7vV9KdAT2eB783KEYqgBWmBTs.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/5Zq1bExwkBIegFx7vV9KdAT2eB783KEYqgBWmBTs.png)

5. Insert your snippet in one of the code text boxes and click on **Save**
![uploads/help-center/images/sZQLCiGQZzlzQ6gIl5Cd0dASNM3cIGS0rkuo9e8m.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/sZQLCiGQZzlzQ6gIl5Cd0dASNM3cIGS0rkuo9e8m.png)

6. Click on **Save** in the main editor page
![uploads/help-center/images/jkGZiCFfdagNzGghJ3ixmIxhhzXRLaR9gcKLBYkH.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/jkGZiCFfdagNzGghJ3ixmIxhhzXRLaR9gcKLBYkH.png)

7. Your AI support agent button will now be displayed on your **odoo** website page
![uploads/help-center/images/94qBjkw5qFjbCo0Ihzicjn2jwULOZqojBOyrHXgU.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/94qBjkw5qFjbCo0Ihzicjn2jwULOZqojBOyrHXgU.png)

8. If you click on the button it will display the AI support agent dialogue
![uploads/help-center/images/wRlfsoHI2Q5Gv54g8rPg5a7AJbPBOdSVeZVLHviM.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/wRlfsoHI2Q5Gv54g8rPg5a7AJbPBOdSVeZVLHviM.png)

Your AI support agent has now been added to your **odoo** website and your visitors/customers can start interacting with it!
