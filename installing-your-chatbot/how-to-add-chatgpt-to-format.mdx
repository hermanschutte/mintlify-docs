You want to embed your custom trained AI support agent in your **Format** website page. Here's how to add an AI support agent to Format with SiteSpeakAI.

**Format** is a website builder that focuses on **visual simplicity and speed**. Unlike some builders that require a lot of coding or dragging and dropping complex elements, Format uses a **"block-based" system**. You essentially stack and arrange pre-designed blocks (like text, images, buttons, forms, videos, etc.) to build your website. 

**Website:** [https://format.com/](https://format.com/)

## Add your AI support agent to your **Format** website

### In SiteSpeakAI

Log into your SiteSpeakAI account and select your agent from the dropdown menu at the top, click on the **Install Agent** section in the sidebar, select the **Javascript** tab and then click on the **Copy Code** button to copy your agent code snippet.

![uploads/help-center/images/H4wvj3M3Jn9JIAP6J7wWKTLWoCa05t6BbCZFpF4W.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/H4wvj3M3Jn9JIAP6J7wWKTLWoCa05t6BbCZFpF4W.png)

### In Format

1. Log into your **Format** account and select **Dashboard** (if not already selected)

![uploads/help-center/images/gIyABoLJpc8QmeSngyhMhbMMlfpe6LPRYVbhjwck.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/gIyABoLJpc8QmeSngyhMhbMMlfpe6LPRYVbhjwck.png)

2. Click on **Settings** in the left side menu

![uploads/help-center/images/kwS3VG7dWerLsLuErLqC0WHHN0zV1wPokHfqZkci.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/kwS3VG7dWerLsLuErLqC0WHHN0zV1wPokHfqZkci.png)

3. Under **Site Settings**, scroll down to `Custom <head> Include`, insert your agent snippet in the provided text box and click  on **Save Changes**

![uploads/help-center/images/5pfhv72Jc36BvSfxG8s7wMe665YYvfIXITC9Ppy2.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/5pfhv72Jc36BvSfxG8s7wMe665YYvfIXITC9Ppy2.png)

4. Your AI support agent button will now be displayed on your **Format** website page

![uploads/help-center/images/W3fuYCbTF5vHMssmhHm2FjeLqzIdaNsYwa2QCJCY.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/W3fuYCbTF5vHMssmhHm2FjeLqzIdaNsYwa2QCJCY.png)

5. If you click on the button it will display the AI support agent dialogue

![uploads/help-center/images/bBLlOMg8F98wZGswy63nJLHeXdUDImh2afllZXte.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/bBLlOMg8F98wZGswy63nJLHeXdUDImh2afllZXte.png)

Your AI support agent has now been added to your **Format** website and your visitors/customers can start interacting with it!
