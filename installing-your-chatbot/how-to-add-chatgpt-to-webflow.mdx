You want to embed your custom trained chatbot in your Webflow website. Here's how to add ChatGPT to Webflow with SiteSpeakAI.

## Add your chatbot to your Webflow website

<InstallCopyCode />
 
### In Webflow

Go to the WebFlow page where you want to add your chatbot.

1. Add a new **Webflow Section**

2. Add a new **Webflow Container** inside the Section

3. Add **Embed** inside the **Container**

4. Paste your chatbot code snippet into the Embed modal

5. Save your page, and hit **Preview**

6. Your chatbot is added to your Webflow website
