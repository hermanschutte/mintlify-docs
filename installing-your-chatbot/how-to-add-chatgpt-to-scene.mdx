You want to embed your custom trained chatbot in your **Scene** website page. Here's how to add ChatGPT to Scene with SiteSpeakAI.

## Add your chatbot to your **Scene** website

### In SiteSpeakAI

Select your chatbot from the dropdown menu at the top, click on the **Install Chatbot** section in the sidebar and then click on the **Copy Code** button to copy your code snippet.

![Copy JS code](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/oTZYtG1bIR1XG6PgnNyYvNGxlXwJaleRkxyJ7zBS.png)

### In Scene

1. Log into your **Scene** account and on your dashboard select **Edit in canvas** for the website you want to edit

![Click on Edit in canvas](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/JYpoWtJqcZdKLgRrYlT9CSxdyG91ZM3BkHjVFiam.png)

2. Click on **Blocks B** to display a list of available Blocks to add to your Scene website

![Click on Blocks B](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/iZAILlp8ON5K7ZUm7wOM9F67rm7bxrw7YJVa0Y8j.png)

3. Under the **Blocks** tab, scroll down on the list of blocks until you find **Embeds** (you can also enter **Embeds** in the search option to speed up your search). Drag the **Embeds** block onto your page just above the Box Header

![Drag the Embeds block onto your page](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/SHR7XXPAd6eQsjCoxheSFpnyf2TOeDx3hq3Cpcer.png)

4. Click on **Embed** in the Site map and insert your chatbot snippet in the **Embed HTML code** text box

![Select Embed in Site map and insert your snippet](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/jbBoUQIDHi7zu0nPXhkzvOlYWjrURPMaCamqUqZe.png)

5. Click on **Preview** to preview you page with your chatbot installed

![Click on Preview to preview your page](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/lm2WNZ4edWUobFwqfPTcArJtxCBNL9objENFYlJp.png)

6. Your chatbot button will now be displayed on your **Scene** website page

![Chatbot button on your page](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/Mhu38TdkKSyhfoBpwpYpnz3lS5OjxjfhZ1DIFaUD.png)

7. If you click on the button it will display the chatbot dialogue

![Chatbot dialogue on your page](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/UOvFhjfP7ZOBAWFqPB5chqjnDgaf7Uid7IyDf9lx.png)

Your chatbot is now added to your **Scene** website and your visitors/customers can start interacting with it!
