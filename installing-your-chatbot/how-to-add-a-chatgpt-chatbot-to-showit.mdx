You want to embed your custom trained virtual assistant in your Showit photography website. Here's how to add ChatGPT to Showit with SiteSpeakAI.

## Add your chatbot to your Showit website

### In SiteSpeakAI

After [creating your chatbot](/getting-started/create-your-chatbot), select your chatbot from the dropdown menu at the top, click on the **Install Chatbot** section in the sidebar, then click on the **Copy Code** button to copy your code snippet.

![CleanShot 2024-01-11 at 14.42.45@2x.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/jhIz4P2sPZRuRjQXMJMswRotQ0lkwavW61O0BcdA.png)

### In Showit

To add your customer support chatbot to your Showit website you will need to make use of the Custom Code feature in Showit. This requires you to have an active subscription.

1. Select the site you would like to edit.

2. Select the **Home page**.

3. Open the **Advanced Settings** tab in the right sidebar and click on the **Custom Head HTML** field to enter your code snippet.

![CleanShot 2024-01-11 at 14.31.56@2x.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/FSs4fmNULkDQxevxMD1oWKSQwB6aK5MZviWe9cCh.png)

4. Paste the code snippet you copied in SiteSpeakAI into the custom head HTML section and click on the **Save** button.

![CleanShot 2024-01-11 at 14.39.21@2x.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/EiTUAlnsESKzNQ4JiRrHUxgkILdOryu44AtSjxDp.png)

5. Click the **Preview** or **Publish** buttons to update your site and view your chatbot.

![CleanShot 2024-01-11 at 14.40.00@2x.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/LdK7qLgjlFao92EQyHlHFtVhxn3Ixpa44tGSaRQx.png)

![CleanShot 2024-01-11 at 14.40.47@2x.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/0G40xIVAYfZPhWgOaMtr1Cw6xxT6xZy7ehtN6nKk.png)

Your Showit website now has a custom trained GPT chatbot that can assist your visitors with more information about your services and help them make bookings.
