You want to embed your custom trained chatbot in your **Photler** website page. Here's how to add ChatGPT to Photler with SiteSpeakAI.

## Add your chatbot to your **Photler** website

### In SiteSpeakAI

Select your chatbot from the dropdown menu at the top, click on the **Install Chatbot** section in the sidebar and then click on the **Copy Code** button to copy your code snippet.

![0 - Photler - Copy Code (cuppadev).png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/9aui2XTEMemGUxr5xCNblj5J8yvXwSjCWjUTFjY0.png)

### In Photler

1. Log into your **Photler** website and on your dashboard, select **SETTINGS** on the left sidebar.

![1 - Photler - Select SETTINGS on your dashboard.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/fKcaTvLkudqfA5ZK5QPUjC12kWJD60ojgMB6dBY6.png)

2. Select **Site settings**.

![2 - Photler - Select Site settings.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/oJYdH9DkSMUN6uwTnjDYtkgm2QdccWzK2YGkmikR.png)

3. Scroll down on the SITE SETTINGS list and insert your snippet under **Custom code**. Click on **SAVE** when done.

![3 - Photler - Insert your snippet.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/1FVBZT4Com6392lcICTyQ30iUfMQdmyDLecILDU8.png)

4. Your chatbot button will now be displayed on your **Photler** website.

![4 - Photler - Chatbot button.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/bNV0zz0CMtLhBwW79VBPAqJPpVj1zgGUH8W3uKnf.png)

5. If you click on the button it will display your chatbot dialogue.

![5 - Photler - Chatbot dialogue.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/nOJJ9Rg6c2lNe9Pz8tbvQZI8ZGxGdswNFQJGoH9z.png)

Your chatbot is now added to your **Photler** website and your customers/visitors can start interacting with it!
