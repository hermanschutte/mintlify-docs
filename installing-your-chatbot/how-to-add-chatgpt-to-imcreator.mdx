You want to embed your custom trained AI support agent in your **imcreator** website page. Here's how to add an AI support agent to imcreator with SiteSpeakAI.

**Imcreator** is a no-code website builder focused on creating visually stunning, interactive websites quickly and easily. It's particularly known for its drag-and-drop interface, pre-built templates, and powerful animation capabilities, making it a good choice for portfolios, landing pages, and small business websites.

**Website URL:** [https://imcreator.com/](https://imcreator.com/)

## Add your AI support agent to your **imcreator** website

### In SiteSpeakAI

Log into your SiteSpeakAI account and select your agent from the dropdown menu at the top, click on the **Install Agent** section in the sidebar, select the **Javascript** tab and then click on the **Copy Code** button to copy your agent code snippet.

![uploads/help-center/images/v2PiDdiF1nqZuGO18phKof6cUQMQ4BmkswSGEcn0.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/v2PiDdiF1nqZuGO18phKof6cUQMQ4BmkswSGEcn0.png)

### In imcreator

1. Log in to your **imcreator** website builder dashboard and click on **My account**

![uploads/help-center/images/LIgFOeqZGOPEtdD9E3RqFOT7yztNmWt6L57D9Nal.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/LIgFOeqZGOPEtdD9E3RqFOT7yztNmWt6L57D9Nal.png)

2. Select your website

![uploads/help-center/images/DubC6FQ9Vxrp8h7Z3DnMtiFktWe0m08Lh78EEVe8.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/DubC6FQ9Vxrp8h7Z3DnMtiFktWe0m08Lh78EEVe8.png)

3. Select **Pro settings** under **Settings**

![uploads/help-center/images/CJJHiN5JsxEliZ0Yd9ipg95HRZZQHcUp0if8Jkj7.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/CJJHiN5JsxEliZ0Yd9ipg95HRZZQHcUp0if8Jkj7.png)

4. Insert your snippet under **Site header code** and click on **Save**

![uploads/help-center/images/NzpdmhnlDfbkoH17WRRWqZtHXyZgYm5o0nypXsUP.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/NzpdmhnlDfbkoH17WRRWqZtHXyZgYm5o0nypXsUP.png)

5. Click on **Publish** to publish your website

![uploads/help-center/images/xK1AeMwNqZjQrDRQ9ZOZM22g6bf7qdJiZeTkOLnZ.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/xK1AeMwNqZjQrDRQ9ZOZM22g6bf7qdJiZeTkOLnZ.png)

6. Your AI support agent button will now be displayed on your **imcreator** website page

![uploads/help-center/images/v2yYY34lIwiCoAw59rUGSFjxc7a1SLSRgX7Yhhnz.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/v2yYY34lIwiCoAw59rUGSFjxc7a1SLSRgX7Yhhnz.png)

7. If you click on the button it will display the AI support agent dialogue

![uploads/help-center/images/qSDPp8d6O8qGZ95vGnNr4ISDLu52FWEG6wynykat.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/qSDPp8d6O8qGZ95vGnNr4ISDLu52FWEG6wynykat.png)

Your AI support agent has now been added to your **imcreator** website and your visitors/customers can start interacting with it!
