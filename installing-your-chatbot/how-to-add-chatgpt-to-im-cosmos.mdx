You want to embed your custom trained AI support agent in your **IM COSMOS** website page. Here's how to add an AI support agent to IM COSMOS with SiteSpeakAI.

**IM Cosmos** is a next-generation modular website builder that merges the power of CMS-based platforms like WordPress with the ease of visual, drag-and-drop design. It's designed as an "operating system" for your online presence, offering AI-assisted onboarding, a scalable CMS, e-commerce tools, and the freedom to host your site anywhere.

Website: [**IM Cosmos**](https://cosmos.imcreator.com/).

## Add your AI support agent to your **IM COSMOS** website

### In SiteSpeakAI

Log into your **SiteSpeakAI** account and select you agent from the dropdown menu at the top, click on the **Install Agent** section in the sidebar, select the **Javascript** tab and then click on the **Copy Code** button to copy your agent code snippet.
![Copy code snippet](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/wDm4GXRxLAPCB4IRRSUkCLvdlmPpaMwf2wmUhOGP.png)

### In IM COSMOS

1. Log into your **IM Cosmos** account and on the dashboard, select your website from the **My Sites** dropdown list
![Select website](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/phcN1Sf4qFwSDeBwhdG9wwqwJhlaut73DFa0i3a2.png)

2. Select the **Website Editor** option
![Website Editor](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/3qqntZGkJUwXn6aQa8G5b3XdN81cAkXHuxycHBT8.png)

3. In the Editor, click on the hamburger menu
![Hamburger menu](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/9qME9J4MMFcFTyRdxBkYyGk8yyUjoAUaek2wm2Em.png)

4. Scroll down on the menu list and select **Advanced**
![Select Advanced](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/En8IOiQ4v91ARyliJBl1M6j0DegxZSfT9sX53oVw.png)

5. Under Advanced, select **Code**
![Select Code](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/XQIwUrv3wUZjV7o7fT2SSpaG45KVKIJ0358PYozD.png)

6. Insert your **AI support agent** code snippet in the text box under **Head Code** and close the Advanced edit option
![Insert snippet](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/JzRh8swbMR9lJihCOJon6VoQd7iikGPqYGbjA9g1.png)

7. Your AI support agent button will now be displayed on your **IM COSMOS** website page
![Chatbot button](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/793NIjgC1tktqLXK0DgmJ2fdxPHKhU29qiDi8nMh.png)

8. If you click on the button it will display the AI support agent dialogue
![Chatbot dialogue](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/H8s1zmpikRcKc2wjTCOz3kuMDpNhoi9h3RsDUrfW.png)

Your AI support agent has now been added to your **IM COSMOS** website and your visitors/customers can start interacting with it!
