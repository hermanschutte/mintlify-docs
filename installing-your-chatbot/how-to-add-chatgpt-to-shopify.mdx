You want to embed your custom trained chatbot in your Shopify store. Here's how to add ChatGPT to Shopify with SiteSpeakAI.

## Add your chatbot to your Shopify store

<InstallCopyCode />
 
### In Shopify

1. Under **Sales Channels**, select **Themes** on the **Online Store** list and click on **Customize** for the relevant theme

![Shopify - 1. Select Themes under Online Store.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/xI00lKq3eU59HasNNMT8veKBgNtfusDJe6eM51FH.png)

2. Click on **Add Section** under **Header** and select **\</> Custom Liquid**

![Shopify - 2. Add Section and select Custom Liquid.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/YqZbK1YHKesotdoxVFxediZf0YfI7dAxmZuB3svK.png)

3. Click on the newly created **Custom Liquid** section

![Shopify - 3. Click on new Custom Liquid Section.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/Ehi8Cun0krairfwPOnDNUXkrDFAkyMWggbelNzEW.png)

4. Insert your snippet in the **Custom Liquid** text box modal and click on **Save**

![Shopify - 4. Insert snippet in new Custom Liquid text box modal and click Save.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/D83tg1yuQ8GClbDa9NFMnYb5EZ4LQtmVXDgVMUCJ.png)

5. That's it - your chatbot is now added to your Shopify store and the chatbot button will appear on your store page

![Shopify - 5. Chatbot button on page.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/voQ3cMdUj1Abu8xd6tsGNquBOtp1oGIb7alJ66bS.png)

6. If you click on the button it will display the chatbot dialogue

![Shopify - 6. Chatbot dialogue on page.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/YZ9QyqjYqNdU8E141m8rSmhIMw9waIX8rYY6fufP.png)

Your customers can now start interacting with your chatbot.
