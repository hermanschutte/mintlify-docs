You want to embed your custom trained AI support agent in your **Waveon** website page. Here's how to add an AI support agent to Waveon with SiteSpeakAI.

**Waveon** is a website builder focused on visual, drag-and-drop simplicity, particularly strong for creating portfolio websites, landing pages, and simple online stores. It's designed to be incredibly easy to use, even for people with *no* prior website building experience. It's a significant departure from traditional, code-heavy website builders.

**Website:** [**Waveon**](https://waveon.com/)

## Add your chatbot to your **Waveon** website

### In SiteSpeakAI

Log into your SiteSpeakAI account and select your agent from the dropdown menu at the top, click on the **Install Agent** section in the sidebar, select the **JavaScript** tab and then click on the **Copy Code** button to copy your agent code snippet.

![Copy code snippet](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/BFYdvea1iBS7RkDfq8XHSEMhbQ0Bq5TDluI3FoEt.png)

### In Waveon

1. Log into your **Waveon** account and select your project on the dashboard
![1 - waveon - Select project.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/GFYK4nqJCN33bpQuBrWSV0vHm3KHWzxweXfbcSmu.png)

2. Click on **Settings** on the left-side menu bar
![2 - waveon - Click  on Settings.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/I0bBOkn9gCPWGw9QWcgRmtTjFCbZklErYhos1rd5.png)

3. Select **Script settings**, insert your snippet in the **head script** input text box and click on **save**. Click on **Post it** to publish your changes.
![3 - waveon - Insert your snippet and post it.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/GdgJdhoN86f7VIwieb566TFJYkoKt2MBfsZp8hNP.png)

4. Your AI support agent button will now be displayed on your **Waveon** website page
![4 - waveon - Chatbot button.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/TtVIFkMRcWUHVwgFxeeQIorOaoY0CrzBydTbEA2M.png)

5. If you click on the button it will display the AI support agent dialogue
![5 - waveon - Chatbot dialogue.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/QIzdqICeWDdh66RNtjhBPQfexfEqYvf4i4ap55A7.png)

Your AI support agent has now been added to your **Waveon** website and your visitors/customers can start interacting with it!
