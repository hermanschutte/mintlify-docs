You want to embed your custom trained AI support agent in your **Plasmic** website page. Here's how to add an AI support agent to Plasmic with SiteSpeakAI.

Plasmic is a visual development platform that helps teams build and manage websites and web applications. 

You can learn more at: [https://www.plasmic.io/](https://www.plasmic.io/)

## Add your AI support agent to your **Plasmic** website

### In SiteSpeakAI

Log into your SiteSpeakAI account and select your agent from the dropdown menu at the top, click on the **Install Agent** section in the sidebar, select the **Javascript** tab and then click on the **Copy Code** button to copy your agent code snippet.
![0-Copy-code-(MuconSA)](/images/installing-your-chatbot/plasmic/0-Copy-code-(MuconSA).png)

### In Plasmic

1. Log into your **Plasmic** account and on your dashboard select the project you want to add your chatbot to
![1-Plasmic-Log-into-Plasmic](/images/installing-your-chatbot/plasmic/1-Plasmic-Log-into-Plasmic.png)

2. Click on **+** to add a new component
![2-Plasmic-Add-new-component](/images/installing-your-chatbot/plasmic/2-Plasmic-Add-new-component.png)

3. Select **Default components** on the left sidebar
![3-Plasmic-Select-Default-components](/images/installing-your-chatbot/plasmic/3-Plasmic-Select-Default-components.png)

4. Scroll down on the list of Default components and select **Embed HTML** in the HTML group
![4-Plasmic-Select-Embed-HTML](/images/installing-your-chatbot/plasmic/4-Plasmic-Select-Embed-HTML.png)

5. Click on the **Code** input box under **Embed HTML props**
![5-Plasmic-Click-on-Code-input-box](/images/installing-your-chatbot/plasmic/5-Plasmic-Click-on-Code-input-box.png)

6. Insert your support agent snippet into the **Code** input box and click on **Save**
![6-Plasmic-Insert-snippet-in-Code-input-box-and-click-on-Save](/images/installing-your-chatbot/plasmic/6-Plasmic-Insert-snippet-in-Code-input-box-and-click-on-Save.png)

7. Click on **Publish** to publish your changes
![7-Plasmic-Click-on-Publish-to-publish-your-changes](/images/installing-your-chatbot/plasmic/7-Plasmic-Click-on-Publish-to-publish-your-changes.png)

8. Your AI support agent button will now be displayed on your **Plasmic** website page
![8-Plasmic-Your-chatbot-button](/images/installing-your-chatbot/plasmic/8-Plasmic-Your-chatbot-button.png)

9. If you click on the button it will display the AI support agent dialogue
![9-Plasmic-Your-chatbot-dialogue](/images/installing-your-chatbot/plasmic/9-Plasmic-Your-chatbot-dialogue.png)

Your AI support agent has now been added to your **Plasmic** website and your visitors/customers can start interacting with it!
