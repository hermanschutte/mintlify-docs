You want to embed your custom trained chatbot in your **iCreate** landing page. Here's how to add ChatGPT to iCreate with SiteSpeakAI.

[iCreate Editor](https://icreate.marketing/) is a no-code marketing platform that lets users build landing pages, surveys, digital catalogs, and more. It includes features like real-time CRM syncing, audience segmentation, and secure hosting, ideal for marketers looking to personalize and automate their campaigns.

For more info see [iCreate Editor](https://icreate.marketing/).

## Add your chatbot to your **iCreate** landing page

### In SiteSpeakAI

Select your chatbot from the dropdown menu at the top, click on the **Install Chatbot** section in the sidebar and then click on the **Copy Code** button to copy your code snippet.

![Copy Code](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/TEmelfYAgcgbmtSA39pOD3MYxnzIfHPgpL2lKjLw.png)

### In iCreate

1. Log into your **iCreate** account and on the dashboard, select **Digital Assets**

![Select Digital Assets](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/Zp5xd91REOsVqAbWxHEL1rcCz4s3jVm0gM1sVtim.png)

2. Under **My Assets**, select **Landing Page** and select the **Editor** option on the **Actions** menu to open your Campaign's landing page to add your chatbot button to

![Select Editor](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/5GlQ8oUQeplPxPswCcDbPRF3Wu3yYwINbugkSK0y.png)

3. Select the **HTML** element on the **External Code** menu on the Editor toolbar

![Select HTML under External Code](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/u6TCmoauaERIthn1jWnTT0UHX6SDLJHAkvzU2Br6.png)

4. Insert your snippet in the HTML Box and click on **OK**

![Insert your snippet and click on OK](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/bzsBK0zM0DXTcd0B7adhe0FGaHVGmizVpzUk7I9t.png)

5. Click on **Save** to save your changes and then click on **Publish** to publish your modified page

![Click on Save and Publish](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/zamIN8bDB9lAU9mkbzIpvMC6L3KS1FuffzDjFUWX.png)

6. Your chatbot button will now be displayed on your **iCreate** landing page

![Chatbot button](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/cgjpO4sm0AV8JcO3aMNTw0psxYD2cQu0yG8CmzCs.png)

7. If you click on the button it will display the chatbot dialogue

![Chatbot dialogue](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/xc6teMTwNuG9sY0h4CEwcbDCDrTogSdBCsjCMRLh.png)

Your chatbot is now added to your **iCreate** landing page and your visitors/customers can start interacting with it!
