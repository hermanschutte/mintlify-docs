You want to embed your custom trained chatbot to your SitePad Builder website. Here's how to add ChatGPT to SitePad Builder with SiteSpeakAI.

## Add your chatbot to your **SitePad Builder** website

<InstallCopyCode />

### In SitePad Builder

1.	Log in to your **SitePad Builder** website

2.	On your dashboard select **Pages** -> **All Pages** in the left sidebar menu

![2 - SitePad Builder - Select Pages on left sidebar menu.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/6HAigkmL9CRYEybG24ztMUugbHApt6qkfNmGQiNw.png)

3. Click on the **Edit this item** icon for the page you want to add your snippet to

![3 - SitePad Builder - Select Edit this item for the page you want to add your snipper to.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/30gxdeC63B3zJEL96s7mmdgsFHWNUqFPPKJVrQqG.png)

4. This will display the selected page in edit mode. Click on the **X** to close the **Edit Page Setting** menu

![4 - SitePad Builder - Click on X to close the Edit Page Setting menu.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/i9FgM5DwL6068cxPvaKHOheAA0288w0JoyYIcZFh.png)

5. A list of available widgets will be displayed under **Widgets**

![5 - SitePad Builder - List of avaiable widgets displayed.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/1HLaWGFzulMMIqHx7ea8PCWGWObtDVw5Vr9SN1gs.png)

6. Scroll down on the list of **Widgets** to **OTHER** and drag the **\</> Embed** widget onto your webpage

![6 - SitePad Builder - Scroll down on the list of avaiable widgets to OTHER and drag the Embed widget onto your page.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/nov4IiMeW8r1A7yFPAoIxz9KnWD0GFjdk48pnVFn.png)

7. Insert your snippet under **Settings** and click on **Update**

![7 - SitePad Builder - Insert snippet and click on Update.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/DlISyZCgphtTWjRK4I2cIG4cFpxWcnngaCf4uYut.png)

8. Your chatbot button will now be displayed on your webpage

![8 - SitePad Builder - Your chatbot button displays on your page..png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/McTGJDm17IIb2qIZo8k9PEZeCwCxrwmAHiBt8euN.png)

9. If you click on the button it will display the chatbot dialogue

![9 - SitePad Builder - Your chatbot dialogue displays on your page.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/RH40TCBUeofinzTeOhZQfDkjEm1YlVtmvCAivqrW.png)

Your chatbot is now added to your **SitePad Builder** webpage and your customers can start interacting with it!
