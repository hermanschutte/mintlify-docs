You want to embed your custom trained AI support agent in your **Jimdo** website page. Here's how to add an AI support agent to Jimdo with SiteSpeakAI.

**Jimdo** is a user-friendly website builder that's particularly popular for small businesses and individuals who want a simple, attractive website without needing extensive technical skills. It's known for its drag-and-drop interface, a variety of pre-designed templates, and its ease of use, often described as "website in minutes." It offers both free and paid plans.

**Website URL:** [https://www.jimdo.com/](https://www.jimdo.com/)

## Add your AI support agent to your **Jimdo** website

### In SiteSpeakAI

Log into your SiteSpeakAI account and select your agent from the dropdown menu at the top, click on the **Install Agent** section in the sidebar, select the **Javascript** tab and then click on the **Copy Code** button to copy your agent code snippet.

![uploads/help-center/images/iEjYAs5dcyHR3zOsUP6wDWh44kCNpSjIZ5zgcRiS.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/iEjYAs5dcyHR3zOsUP6wDWh44kCNpSjIZ5zgcRiS.png)

### In Jimdo

1. Log into your Jimdo account and on your dashboard select **Edit Website**

![uploads/help-center/images/zRygfz5qOiukGdImNeUDZnU0OIeP7IOekSyhc33S.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/zRygfz5qOiukGdImNeUDZnU0OIeP7IOekSyhc33S.png)

2. Select **Menu** to open the **Website editor**

![uploads/help-center/images/qub6teheQvt60ingmK8Wi6v9OBanIke1lWDqhWWH.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/qub6teheQvt60ingmK8Wi6v9OBanIke1lWDqhWWH.png)

3. Select **Settings** on the Website editor tool menu

![uploads/help-center/images/EeI3bx6u0dU1kL8RobxDFM70bi1JIxHSVCnZStWE.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/EeI3bx6u0dU1kL8RobxDFM70bi1JIxHSVCnZStWE.png)

4. Select **Edit Head** under Settings to open the Head editor modal

![uploads/help-center/images/0pslOkm9c3E0KLjkSPCi4oyXrvBfMr6IGjpCY4uB.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/0pslOkm9c3E0KLjkSPCi4oyXrvBfMr6IGjpCY4uB.png)

5. Insert your AI support agent snippet in the Head modal and click on **Save**. Select the **WEBSITE** tab to add the AI support agent to the entire website or the **PAGES** tabe for specific Pages

![uploads/help-center/images/u1YMYC4vzUO4ExP0lROTiScUkshtxph1I51Zk3Hl.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/u1YMYC4vzUO4ExP0lROTiScUkshtxph1I51Zk3Hl.png)

6. Your AI support agent button will now be displayed on your **Jimdo** website page

![uploads/help-center/images/5ScW9WW36dww3ywJ232qI3XdZC8ZLyZA2SaGwYba.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/5ScW9WW36dww3ywJ232qI3XdZC8ZLyZA2SaGwYba.png)

7. If you click on the button it will display the AI support agent dialogue

![uploads/help-center/images/XyTJ1giGLzQBjzZr58XZM2hZoTYx5VK51lFk5Jij.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/XyTJ1giGLzQBjzZr58XZM2hZoTYx5VK51lFk5Jij.png)

Your AI support agent has now been added to your **Jimdo** website and your visitors/customers can start interacting with it!
