You want to embed your custom trained chatbot in your **SITEJET** website page. Here's how to add ChatGPT to SITEJET with SiteSpeakAI.

## Add your chatbot to your **SITEJET** website

<InstallCopyCode />

### In SITEJET

1. Log in to your **SITEJET** account and on your dashboard, click on the menu (**...**) for the website you want to edit
![2 - sitejet.io - Dashboard - select website to edit.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/z0oUEnd2LeebcLv6woGscezlpPrSoYVSyI1eWl8I.png)

2. Select **Edit website**
![3 - sitejet.io - Dashboard - select Edit website to edit your website.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/43v3g1WJJL36mpG8NuPguL6P9dS6qD01eqsZwTTQ.png)

3. Click on **Elements**
![4 - sitejet.io - Editor - click on Elements.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/jKQggoA8TPqwn2tYyFSsNUYzbiQIzKUlfhnD6jhf.png)

4. Drag the **\</> HTML** element onto your website page
![5 - sitejet.io - Editor - drag the HTML Element onto your website page.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/HsiiTbLhXUg2BhoEseWRW1mgYLSreLthoZK1mZg9.png)

5. Insert your snippet and click on **Save**
![6 - sitejet.io - Editor -Insert your snippet & click on Save.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/IlNiQog1O8bqgRA5a0mTbOqOXomNPcmJ7GeU8KO5.png)

6. Your chatbot button will now be displayed on your website page
![7 - sitejet.io - Chatbot - Chatbot button displayed on page.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/qGfkbUSMLHoMdZ3GUDmd3Lih17hqQanK7lcAAUSk.png)

7. If you click on the button it will display the chatbot dialogue
![8 - sitejet.io - Chatbot - Chatbot dialogue displayed on page.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/vcCVAcV7RK0HLKfyJL3wMeZ6gJfsfMR62xHLpOO2.png)

Your chatbot is now added to your **SITEJET** website and your customers can start interacting with it!
