You want to embed your custom trained AI support agent in your **Brizy Cloud** website page. Here's how to add an AI support agent to Brizy Cloud with SiteSpeakAI.

**Brizy Cloud** is a drag-and-drop website builder that's designed to be super easy to use, even if you have *no* coding experience. Think of it like building with LEGOs â€“ you grab pre-made blocks (called "blocks") like text boxes, images, buttons, and more, and simply drag and drop them onto your page to create your website. 

**Website:** [**Brizy Cloud** ](https://brizy.io/)

## Add your AI support agent to your **Brizy Cloud** website

### In SiteSpeakAI

Log into your SiteSpeakAI account and select your agent from the dropdown menu at the top, click on the **Install Agent** section in the sidebar, select the **Javascript** tab and then click on the **Copy Code** button to copy your agent code snippet.

![uploads/help-center/images/i0TJNFSNthTsKDANtxvN58VqdHCEiq8awlPUEPDP.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/i0TJNFSNthTsKDANtxvN58VqdHCEiq8awlPUEPDP.png)

### In Brizy Cloud

1. Log into your **Brizy Cloud** account and on the dashboard, select the project you want to add your AI support agent to

![uploads/help-center/images/xmAfnQZCpBnrGZkz5OzS7mdQVsXMMflt6IGW1QPD.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/xmAfnQZCpBnrGZkz5OzS7mdQVsXMMflt6IGW1QPD.png)

2. Select the **CMS** item on the left sidebar

![uploads/help-center/images/mY4795HBBbt95xNASHjugeya5LtouMgNVB0OLSQU.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/mY4795HBBbt95xNASHjugeya5LtouMgNVB0OLSQU.png)

3. Click on **Project Settings**

![uploads/help-center/images/ncKFYxbyOJDKgpp3dkLK4EySVUAc0iEotvZsh572.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/ncKFYxbyOJDKgpp3dkLK4EySVUAc0iEotvZsh572.png)

4. Select the **\</> CODE** option tab

![uploads/help-center/images/tXzH0tV80yM3fVY7IkxtxuqY8ifU35qlMSPd36oU.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/tXzH0tV80yM3fVY7IkxtxuqY8ifU35qlMSPd36oU.png)

5. Insert your AI support agent snippet code under **Code injection > Header** and click on **Save Changes** to save your code

![uploads/help-center/images/uKYA603IssKTyAExnR6hXXiggzJLdGOiTHPpCDTu.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/uKYA603IssKTyAExnR6hXXiggzJLdGOiTHPpCDTu.png)

6. Close **Settings** to return to your website

![uploads/help-center/images/oqAE8h9yaDlMTc13CKnoejvQKVa1kxLMfp1VPaII.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/oqAE8h9yaDlMTc13CKnoejvQKVa1kxLMfp1VPaII.png)

7. Click on **Preview** to preview your webpage

![uploads/help-center/images/X7xADfdMI5QK3IBp5qPWYFsrp7EodeYyBkrnTVkb.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/X7xADfdMI5QK3IBp5qPWYFsrp7EodeYyBkrnTVkb.png)

8. Your AI support agent button will now be displayed on your **Brizy Cloud** website page

![uploads/help-center/images/geMbYzuZ61UhF4iFzkUcaXJqOYY39COkBMCxdopJ.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/geMbYzuZ61UhF4iFzkUcaXJqOYY39COkBMCxdopJ.png)

9. If you click on the button it will display the AI support agent dialogue

![uploads/help-center/images/5Ohxrh0rcvAWos3efX97NXRKUNIbU2GZAudDL7HH.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/5Ohxrh0rcvAWos3efX97NXRKUNIbU2GZAudDL7HH.png)

Your AI support agent has now been added to your **Brizy Cloud** website and your visitors/customers can start interacting with it!
