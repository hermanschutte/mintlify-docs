You want to embed your custom trained AI support agent in your **Pagewiz** website page. Here's how to add an AI support agent to Pagewiz with SiteSpeakAI.

**Pagewiz** is a landing page platform that enables marketers to create, publish, and optimize high-converting landing pages with ease. It offers a wizard-style interface that feels as straightforward as a PowerPoint presentation, helping users launch campaigns faster and smarter. The platform handles hosting, performance monitoring, and maintenance around the clock, reducing the overhead associated with managing your conversion funnel.

**Website:** [**Pagewiz**](https://www.pagewiz.com/).

## Add your chatbot to your **Pagewiz** website

### In SiteSpeakAI

Log into your **SiteSpeakAI** account and select your agent from the dropdown menu at the top, click on the **Install Agent** section in the sidebar, select the **Javascript** tab and then click on the **Copy Code** button to copy your agent code snippet.
![Copy code snippet](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/X06HyME61qsre0nnGqMzZFjj4M39dta1DGfuJ8Lp.png)

### In Pagewiz

1. Log into your **Pagewiz** account, make sure **Pages** is selected on the top menu bar and click on **Design** for the page you want to add your chatbot to
![1 - Pagewiz - Dashboard, select page.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/qaXZzNet48UM3Y9OSrrfnfIAJlb0mOYjErIOcjof.png)

**Note**: There are two (2) ways to add your snippet to your **Pagewiz** page. 

a. Using the **Embed Code** component to insert the code per page or

b. Add a global **JS Script** which will then be applied to all pages

#### A. Using the ***Embed Code*** component option:

i. Drag the **Embed Code** component onto your page
![2 - Pagewiz - Drag Embed Code.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/ptZ2fTEkJUf2AwamOhTjH07wTHytzQRhohsTjmXp.png)

ii. Enter a **name** for the code component, insert your snippet in the **HTML code** text box and click on **Apply**
![3 - Pagewiz - Insert your snippet.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/PKfDF8FGai9YQY8wRawSfIvVeE2stpuGIehIchVk.png)

iii. Click on **Save** to save your page modifications
![4 - Pagewiz - Save Embed Code option.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/ZXboXnOwKhDwDMFGDNE6jrdynmrJLADXAVcX0CZI.png)

#### B. Using the global ***JS Script*** option:

i. Select **Scripts & Styles** on the top menu bar and select the **JS Scripts** option
![5 - Pagewiz - Select JS Scripts.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/dvJkEThOHfwndLlNhvtr6vxnzpiIFNykIWx91aWT.png)

ii. Click on **Add new script**
![6 - Pagewiz - Add new script.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/0szUP0612mJuHYWGdqDVFNNNH0PvXtnUw06DEqAJ.png)

iii. Select the **location** to place your script (**Head**, **Body** or **Footer**), provide a **name** for your script, insert your snippet and click on **Apply**
![7 - Pagewiz - Insert snippet and Apply.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/0UpoZ4Rm9KclUQCUaYrr9PDLsRw3jhFI45wFQqvD.png)

iv. Close the **Scripts** dialogue
![8 - Pagewiz - Close the Scripts dialogue.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/K1wFnLoZfBBQmHWvccvmTJ2onurUahnrcvNeug2z.png)

v. Click on **Save** to save your page modifications 
![9 - Pagewiz - Click on Save.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/NOZoXn7mAdIX7jUxxHEsRTCMaLmzZ3rs8yTSXhWl.png)

#### C. For both options:

1. Your chatbot button will now be displayed on your **Pagewiz** website page
![10 - Pagewiz - Chatbot button.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/zeRV8EkppHDaZcpKp2gYwuj86zF87DsQT1rwv3ho.png)

2. If you click on the button it will display the chatbot dialogue
![11 - Pagewiz - Chatbot dialogue.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/7tJ3jAWW1zyLptXJqIUZW3hf6Kfaxai8b2736j7X.png)

Your AI support agent has now been added to your **Pagewiz** website and your visitors/customers can start interacting with it!
