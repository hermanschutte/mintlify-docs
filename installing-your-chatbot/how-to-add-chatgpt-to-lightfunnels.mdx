You want to embed your custom trained AI support agent in your **Lightfunnels** website page. Here's how to add an AI support agent to Lightfunnels with SiteSpeakAI.

**Lightfunnels** is a no-code platform designed to help entrepreneurs and small businesses quickly build and launch online courses, memberships, and digital products. It focuses on streamlining the entire sales process, from landing pages to payment processing.

**Website:** [https://lightfunnels.com/](https://lightfunnels.com/)

## Add your chatbot to your **Lightfunnels** website

### In SiteSpeakAI

Log into your SiteSpeakAI account and select your agent from the dropdown menu at the top, click on the **Install Agent** section in the sidebar, select the **JavaScript** tab and then click on the **Copy Code** button to copy your agent code snippet.

![Copy Code](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/scfBM5t5AXvJKIbnQxi5Cbgg1IOOOBGSz2LZOt1J.png)

### In Lightfunnels

1. Log into your **Lightfunnels** account and on the dashboard, select **Stores** on the left-side menu bar

![Select Stores](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/oSDTDZCGKikPYPXL6O0EhB1SLJdVWoR7B1YJieLg.png)

2. Select **Open store** to open the store you want to add your AI support agent snippet to

![Click on Open store](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/gaDjFsc0irMWXl2yVLL1Zn8wrGKY0F7upSJ8mSH3.png)

3. Click on the **Settings** tab

![Select Settings](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/iUJ1Olug2xwbqdouV1CkWqDnASZswnXHd2QEoDRm.png)

4. Select **Store template**, insert your snippet in the **Custom scripts** box under **Storefront preferences** and click on **Save** to save your changes

![Insert snippet and Save](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/NcNBXnOmD1c9aCZh4y7stPrcdz85ntYkzieqC3Jq.png)

5. Your AI support agent button will now be displayed on your **Lightfunnels** storefront page

![Chatbot button](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/emOffSwCBqxLzWZtdYKleBnDYkJneWBVdbiwJ5h2.png)

6. If you click on the button it will display the AI support agent dialogue

![Chatbot dialogue](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/h2V3Fsc3n5xUc3H5wvS0kDccTMLDHKqx1zIEiq7N.png)

Your AI support agent has now been added to your **Lightfunnels** store and your visitors/customers can start interacting with it!
