You want to embed your custom trained AI support agent in your **one.com** website page. Here's how to add an AI support agent to one.com with SiteSpeakAI.

**one.com** is a super-easy, all-in-one website builder allowing you to create, launch, and grow your website with easy AI powered tools, built-in marketing features, and expert support, everything included, from day one.

**Website URL:** [https://one.com/](https://one.com/)

## Add your AI support agent to your **one.com** website

### In SiteSpeakAI

Log into your SiteSpeakAI account and select your agent from the dropdown menu at the top, click on the **Install Agent** section in the sidebar, select the **Javascript** tab and then click on the **Copy Code** button to copy your agent code snippet.
![uploads/help-center/images/MAUv4a6REz7lk9nNdyAelDyJsZyLNBUo0fz3MruX.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/MAUv4a6REz7lk9nNdyAelDyJsZyLNBUo0fz3MruX.png)

### In one.com

1. Log in to your **one.com** website builder dashboard and in the left sidebar, select **Code** under **Widgets** and drag the Code widget onto your page in the page Header
![uploads/help-center/images/1RYd0gdXKgrXZDuGCRZgE8sMwmP9cJibzyqJSKGn.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/1RYd0gdXKgrXZDuGCRZgE8sMwmP9cJibzyqJSKGn.png)
![uploads/help-center/images/kH30bJWEh4ZjylNW6Xiif6nZHuQsa5sWePW2kQfE.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/kH30bJWEh4ZjylNW6Xiif6nZHuQsa5sWePW2kQfE.png)

2. Insert your AI support agent snippet in the **Embed code** editor text area and click on **Save**
![uploads/help-center/images/i0i2uFPjEg1YjyeAksWj6OaY3cdIVwnmK2tDZDnz.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/i0i2uFPjEg1YjyeAksWj6OaY3cdIVwnmK2tDZDnz.png)

3. Click on **Preview** to preview your page
![uploads/help-center/images/HGCVNscWa51taHPdCUHeZy7WCyj1sCKGmivzrtKi.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/HGCVNscWa51taHPdCUHeZy7WCyj1sCKGmivzrtKi.png)

4. Your AI support agent button will now be displayed on your **one.com** website page
![uploads/help-center/images/leiX5ADpF5LMWiEjHO3McVsefukZnRR11arndgGa.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/leiX5ADpF5LMWiEjHO3McVsefukZnRR11arndgGa.png)

5. If you click on the button it will display the AI support agent dialogue
![uploads/help-center/images/i4P6lg6h22chTCJICmcuqu5C8orv9ln7CPZsvgCE.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/i4P6lg6h22chTCJICmcuqu5C8orv9ln7CPZsvgCE.png)

Your AI support agent has now been added to your **one.com** website and your visitors/customers can start interacting with it!
