You want to embed your custom trained AI support agent in your **ConcreteCMS** website page. Here's how to add an AI support agent to ConcreteCMS with SiteSpeakAI.

**Concrete CMS** is a powerful, open-source, PHP-based Content Management System (CMS) known for its flexibility, modularity, and focus on developer control. It's particularly well-suited for complex websites, web applications, and digital experiences where you need a high degree of customization and scalability. Unlike some CMS platforms that prioritize ease of use for non-technical users, Concrete CMS is designed for developers who want to build bespoke solutions. It uses a component-based architecture, allowing you to build and reuse content structures and functionality.

**Website:** [**https://www.concretecms.com/** ](https://www.concretecms.com/)

## Add your AI support agent to your **ConcreteCMS** website

### In SiteSpeakAI

Log into your SiteSpeakAI account and select your agent from the dropdown menu at the top, click on the **Install Agent** section in the sidebar, select the **Javascript** tab and then click on the **Copy Code** button to copy your agent code snippet.
![uploads/help-center/images/OJolK9DNzK5xU4m3uEPeL5NHurFcalVEoLQOrMvx.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/OJolK9DNzK5xU4m3uEPeL5NHurFcalVEoLQOrMvx.png)

### In ConcreteCMS

1. Log into your **ConcreteCMS** account and on the dashboard select **View Sites**

![uploads/help-center/images/ngq3oGwcuqQ3flrMy9I8wZLvHE3c4pjznPhxwnbl.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/ngq3oGwcuqQ3flrMy9I8wZLvHE3c4pjznPhxwnbl.png)

2. Select and log in to the site you want to add your AI support agent to

![uploads/help-center/images/Khhc2jrU7urhNYECDdQsjru3Lo6QAgYIDv6WXqri.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/Khhc2jrU7urhNYECDdQsjru3Lo6QAgYIDv6WXqri.png)

3. Select **Edit This Page**

![uploads/help-center/images/6UNMzXYqDiQvXMYQ9CCrdF1lHxg5WBGnSfnAC47r.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/6UNMzXYqDiQvXMYQ9CCrdF1lHxg5WBGnSfnAC47r.png)

4. Click on **SITEWIDE NAVIGATION** and select **Add Block**

![uploads/help-center/images/A3k3cKRSho1sSDWubvdv22yVz7vme3etrY9qRoNk.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/A3k3cKRSho1sSDWubvdv22yVz7vme3etrY9qRoNk.png)

5. Select the **HTML** block to add your AI support agent snippet

![uploads/help-center/images/Gnu2IXeri9GkTzcChUhER24ZVS6IbTbgIv5XLolP.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/Gnu2IXeri9GkTzcChUhER24ZVS6IbTbgIv5XLolP.png)

6. Insert your snippet and click on **Add**

![uploads/help-center/images/55j8d4qTq0tEzUOBsa7tXzRFEdXKbGTQUnRSo0tI.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/55j8d4qTq0tEzUOBsa7tXzRFEdXKbGTQUnRSo0tI.png)

7. Exit **Edit Mode** to **Save** & **Publish** your changes

![uploads/help-center/images/zoqcIyymyRgNpjhKw1xIa065hri40x1EgIXThnCu.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/zoqcIyymyRgNpjhKw1xIa065hri40x1EgIXThnCu.png)

![uploads/help-center/images/ogxdd1WRLocDYdkD75XBeZdDAMVaY1CsJlD82V5f.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/ogxdd1WRLocDYdkD75XBeZdDAMVaY1CsJlD82V5f.png)

8. Your AI support agent button will now be displayed on your **ConcreteCMS** website page

![uploads/help-center/images/yVFHs4vryf70SrNcC7clFc8ulkvB2LjHDbKwWqY1.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/yVFHs4vryf70SrNcC7clFc8ulkvB2LjHDbKwWqY1.png)

9. If you click on the button it will display the AI support agent dialogue

![uploads/help-center/images/XvPJNuRKROvCxx9kuEdb9NbiFitmOG8E4fpu7BMi.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/XvPJNuRKROvCxx9kuEdb9NbiFitmOG8E4fpu7BMi.png)

Your AI support agent has now been added to your **ConcreteCMS** website and your visitors/customers can start interacting with it!
