You want to embed your custom trained chatbot in your **TeleportHQ** website page. Here's how to add ChatGPT to TeleportHQ with SiteSpeakAI.

## Add your chatbot to your **TeleportHQ** website

### In SiteSpeakAI

Select your chatbot from the dropdown menu at the top, click on the **Install Chatbot** section in the sidebar and then click on the **Copy Code** button to copy your code snippet.
![0 - TeleportHQ - Copy js code.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/kU9oXmxJZ7osaL8nqoOKFkdjkUhiFsTjNv1lJdI8.png)

### In TeleportHQ

1. Log in to your **TeleportHQ** account and select the project you want to add your chatbot to
![1 - TeleportHQ - Select your project.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/Iiq5cdj1rE5QvEZmFtk1AuAGBAlKrMtPfw6ToCvd.png)

2. In the **Design Editor** select your project and on the dropdown select **Project Settings**
![2 - TeleportHQ - Select Project Settings.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/4miTAAYk2f41XT5Pu1BOa2sCtVdzF41p9PfT2Hdk.png)

3. Under Project Settings select **Custom Code**
![3 - TeleportHQ - Select Custom Code.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/nP0m4QZwQeBAOrkCUnzMKaGrpAoYmwJ0B7QtZLMj.png)

4. Insert your snippet in the **Head** part and click on **Save Changes** to save your changes
![4 - TeleportHQ - Insert snippet and save changes.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/zPAC1m3Ebzz1Lrfu5Hb2cyOScrbv2rCy1hnBvkQs.png)

5. Click on **Open Editor** to return to the **Design Editor**
![5 - TeleportHQ -Click on Open Editor.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/MC6eYF6FQk2eFsjW3qO1xg28G6RELoOafDZqawQO.png)

6. In the Design Editor click on **Publish** to publish your changes
![6 - TeleportHQ - Click on Publish.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/C8Js0PGXVyonp9daJah02PkkUmeVRZ1A0lvEg6xq.png)

7. **Voila!** Your Chatbot button will now be displayed on your **TeleportHQ** webpage
![7 - TeleportHQ - Chatbot button displays on web page.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/ChsRj0wcJoQPQYSEnDXmoTuZGAQzTGfAuf9TZCsq.png)

8. If you click on the button it will display the Chatbot dialogue
![8 - TeleportHQ - Chatbot dialogue displays on web page.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/60afLxvqpyOHDDx7J3KE5gGZJWMk3y4dnEuOreIW.png)

Your chatbot has now been added to your **TeleportHQ** website and your customers/visitors can start interacting with it!
