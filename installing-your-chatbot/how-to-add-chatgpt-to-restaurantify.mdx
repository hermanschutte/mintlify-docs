You want to embed your custom trained AI support agent in your **Restaurantify** website page. Here's how to add an AI support agent to Restaurantify with SiteSpeakAI.

**Restaurantify** is a website builder specifically designed for restaurants, cafes, and food businesses. It's built around the idea of making it incredibly easy to create a beautiful, mobile-friendly website without needing any coding knowledge. It focuses on key features restaurants need: online ordering, table reservations, menu display, photos, and contact information.  It's a drag-and-drop system, so you can easily add and rearrange elements.

**Website URL:** [**Restaurantify**](https://www.restaurantify.com/)

## Add your AI support agent to your **Restaurantify** website

### In SiteSpeakAI

Log into your SiteSpeakAI account and select your agent from the dropdown menu at the top, click on the **Install Agent** section in the sidebar, select the **Javascript** tab and then click on the **Copy Code** button to copy your agent code snippet.
![uploads/help-center/images/Iyb4ncCndqKcam0DBbsBdeSteXDAWVtUVmtFejy6.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/Iyb4ncCndqKcam0DBbsBdeSteXDAWVtUVmtFejy6.png)

### In Restaurantify

1. Log into your **Restaurantify** account and on the dashboard select **Stores**
![uploads/help-center/images/znd0fWdHvndaFnbaC6XpFIGfNYwtvnzTqrZTLTla.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/znd0fWdHvndaFnbaC6XpFIGfNYwtvnzTqrZTLTla.png)

2. Select **Store Settings** from the the top menu bar and select **All Settings** on the list
![uploads/help-center/images/ctdkgRtazRwyHj4Yt3lzkm0hn6Bx5AZk705gETgD.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/ctdkgRtazRwyHj4Yt3lzkm0hn6Bx5AZk705gETgD.png)

3. On the **Editing** page, scroll down and select **Google Analytics**
![uploads/help-center/images/A5tQOgDLqjsuw4F9bzIr17DnmmBmUoE3wp85an8p.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/A5tQOgDLqjsuw4F9bzIr17DnmmBmUoE3wp85an8p.png)

4. Insert your copied snippet under **Head Content** and click on **Update Store** to update your store with the added snippet
![uploads/help-center/images/f1XK8lOJxapIUtfeDmaJbASss6uojCYLFmhafCBb.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/f1XK8lOJxapIUtfeDmaJbASss6uojCYLFmhafCBb.png)

5. Your AI support agent button will now be displayed on your **Restaurantify** website page
![uploads/help-center/images/FDRCnqoAIVBPDrL1kduVOMaUhIxfwzVcULH5xknW.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/FDRCnqoAIVBPDrL1kduVOMaUhIxfwzVcULH5xknW.png)

6. If you click on the button it will display the AI support agent dialogue
![uploads/help-center/images/vT4JAoTnjEhGGiNlklZTqmUsrkX0lEv4TM1KnHwt.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/vT4JAoTnjEhGGiNlklZTqmUsrkX0lEv4TM1KnHwt.png)

Your AI support agent has now been added to your **Restaurantify** website and your visitors/customers can start interacting with it!
