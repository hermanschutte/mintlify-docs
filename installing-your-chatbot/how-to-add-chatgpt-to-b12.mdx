You want to embed your custom trained AI support agent in your **b12.io** website page. Here's how to add an AI support agent to b12.io with SiteSpeakAI.

**What is b12.io?**

b12.io is a platform that helps you **easily create and manage beautiful, branded email newsletters and landing pages.** Think of it as a simplified way to build professional-looking emails and sign-up forms without needing to be a coding expert. It focuses on making email marketing accessible to small businesses, creators, and anyone who wants to build an email list.

**Website URL:** [https://b12.io/](https://b12.io/)

## Add your AI support agent to your **b12.io** website

### In SiteSpeakAI
Log into your SiteSpeakAI account and select your agent from the dropdown menu at the top, click on the **Install Agent** section in the sidebar, select the **Javascript** tab and then click on the **Copy Code** button to copy your agent code snippet.
![Copy code snippet](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/W6d81XxNT3OQq2w96pdJCzlRqmNtnItZwkIbzITI.png)

### In b12.io

1. Log in to your **b12.io** website builder dashboard and select **Website**

![Select Website](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/dUeyJ2SloisUyIlKvguxOJMWkf9rOK6bNjPRPwXp.png)

2. Select **Style** under **Website**

![Select Style](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/LMWr7rmzJBpKgbUJHkQe22sxtxU32HqG2WSEqkiA.png)

3. Select **Custom HTML** under **Advanced style Options**

![Select Custom HTML](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/gXUx12SvR336UziGmHz2fWrINIs3CkIh684D7eYc.png)

4. Insert your snippet code under **Head HTML** and click on **Save**

![Insert snippet](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/EmXbGfvh0Rvm5vgM2PbOCRENCzoTFFkf4oklpbAq.png)

5. Click on **Preview** or **Publish** to preview your page edits or publish them to your website

![Preview or Publish](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/81uEYC73doOGWENW2KixS65ERYuUKQa9pU81PYsG.png)

6. Your AI support agent button will now be displayed on your **b12.io** website page

![Chatbot button](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/PVcRqD4TxH5c5I7dAMU7VwEytVLPDQDf71KyW2ad.png)

7. If you click on the button it will display the AI support agent dialogue

![Chatbot dialogue](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/9jsSrKq9RqJWVm5ymaV6XswLAayZwx4Brl7W34wc.png)

Your AI support agent has now been added to your **b12.io** website and your visitors/customers can start interacting with it!
