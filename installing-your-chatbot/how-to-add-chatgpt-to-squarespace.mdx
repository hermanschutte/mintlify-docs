You want to embed your custom trained chatbot in your Squarespace website. Here's how to add ChatGPT to Squarespace with SiteSpeakAI.

## Add your chatbot to your Squarespace website

<InstallCopyCode />

### In Squarespace

On your dashboard click on **WEBSITE** for the site you want to edit.

![Squarespace - Dashboard.PNG](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/uVg03JtNJ2Uj4SquVzGtMKEO7TgXmzRUJROgJUkt.png)

1. Under **Pages** select **Website Tools**

![Squarespace - 2. Select Website Tools.PNG](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/tu5cLKhe8EAGBoojSWbCmLK0IAKLr3Gqq3zZpVDy.png)

2. Under **Website Tools** select **Code Injection**

![Squarespace - 3. Select Code Injection.PNG](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/dDGBGl3zU6LS2OY6ckdk2c87NVM3Fgx7IN312i2X.png)

3. Insert your snippet in the **Header** part under **Code Injection** and click on **Save** to save your code

![Squarespace - 4. Enter Code in Header.PNG](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/4PjOIsotOMsGNaqgnG6PGgUfNuWZbxATQRQuF8Cn.png)

4. Click on **Preview** to display a preview of your live website

![Squarespace - 5. Preview.PNG](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/7qi7UOp7RDxmnyWG5UNvgEkVPSfZOmhbXAeHlyjD.png)

5. Your chatbot button will now be displayed on your website page
![Squarespace - 6. Chatbot Button.PNG](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/SsHd61gvTRL3O0538W4jc58XGmDkz25wfdGe7uh8.png)

6. If you click on the button it will display the chatbot dialogue
![Squarespace - 7. Chatbot Dialogue.PNG](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/MoHRMakfTcDh78psMFbLQnlHhCTqBP6AW6nMoRZr.png)

Your chatbot is now added to your Squarespace website and your customers can start interacting with it
