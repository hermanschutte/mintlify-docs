You want to embed your custom trained AI support agent in your **Droip** website page. Here's how to add an AI support agent to Droip with SiteSpeakAI.

**Droip** is a no-code website builder focused on speed and simplicity. It's particularly known for its incredibly fast page building â€“ claiming to build a fully functional website in just 60 seconds. It's designed for entrepreneurs and small businesses who want a quick and easy way to launch a website without needing coding skills. It offers a limited selection of templates and customization options, prioritizing speed over extensive design flexibility.

**Website:** [https://droip.com/](https://droip.com/)

## Add your AI support agent to your **Droip** website

### In SiteSpeakAI

Log into your SiteSpeakAI account and select your agent from the dropdown menu at the top, click on the **Install Agent** section in the sidebar, select the **Javascript** tab and then click on the **Copy Code** button to copy your agent code snippet.
![uploads/help-center/images/cWYyXNV3rJsalreEPjGdOr2m8ToUx2SEXmkkSmJT.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/cWYyXNV3rJsalreEPjGdOr2m8ToUx2SEXmkkSmJT.png)

### In Droip

1. Log into your **Droip** account and select **Pages** on the top menu
![1 - Droip - Select Pages.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/2YxfrfkVnFrtFnxr1zZtNNtMyvNanqH4SzhJJ1Nw.png)

2. Select the page you want to add your agent to
![2 - Droip - Select your page.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/D0ez8y9WSwox0kIh5jsUKudZu3tZqr0DYJTFGG9E.png)

3. Select **Elements** on the top menu bar, scroll down to **Components** and drag the **Code** element onto the Header on your page
![3 - Droip - Drag Code element onto Header.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/V4l1Vrjkd8ombrt0Wxt1mURfb763NrChdF4diBpF.png)

4. Select **More Otions** for the **Code** element
![4 - Droip - Select More Options for the Code element.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/N4XmMdx1XlsNzfGlXd6S9wDgKoJ17AZM93RNsPHa.png)

5. This will display the **Custom Code Editor** allowing you to add your agent snippet
![5 - Droip -This will display the Custom Code Editor.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/wRCfazTfrdN8BaAIqlR9q3hgbmyxxbtEErANuWes.png)

6. Insert your code snippet and click on **Embed**
![6 - Droip -Insert your Code and click on Embed.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/KM5H1MpmAXJWd6sbwZxh6PcbAn7qUSxAMeL5qXG3.png)

7. Click on **Preview** to preview your inserted agent button
![7 - Droip - Click on Preview.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/ogbLAXzPCKxU2aTjVSVocYLjqvNTxBjK20EJW9vp.png)

8. Your AI support agent button will now be displayed on your **Droip** website page
![8 - Droip - Chatbot button on Droip page.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/EV8bApgMWHOIBXBfsRgryMDoHyEhWyJwpUZNVSEG.png)

9. If you click on the button it will display the AI support agent dialogue
![9 - Droip - Chatbot dialogue on Droip page.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/lFQ1KPgOcBFCQg8nFDVp6Fl4OyCzBK68gORj2pEC.png)

Your AI support agent has now been added to your **Droip** website and your visitors/customers can start interacting with it!
