You want to embed your custom trained AI support agent in your **Designmodo** website page. Here's how to add an AI support agent to Designmodo with SiteSpeakAI.

**Designmodo** consists of 3 Apps, i.e. **Postcards**, **Slides** and **Startup**

- **Postcards** - A Powerfully Simple Email Template Builder. **Postcards** is how you design email newsletters that shine, in a fraction of the time, with one-click exports to every ESP [Learn more](https://designmodo.com/postcards/).

- **Slides** - A No-Code Website Builder for Creating Beautiful and Animated Pages. In **Slides**, you can tell an unforgettable story. Bring your website vision to life with smooth animations and bold creative. It is a fast, intuitive website builder to generate beautiful, animated websites without a single line of coding. Choose from hundreds of premade UI blocks or use a template, then just drag and drop them into place [Learn more](https://designmodo.com/slides/).

- **Startup** - A Drag & Drop Bootstrap Template Builder. With **Startup**, every developer is more productive. Publish perfect, responsive landing pages faster, with a simple drag-and-drop interface. It is a platform offering a curated collection of website templates and resources specifically geared towards early-stage startups. It provides a marketplace for designers and developers to find and purchase professionally designed website solutions [Learn more](https://designmodo.com/startup/).

In this tutorial we will look at **Slides** as well as **Startup**

## A. Add your AI support agent to your Designmodo website using the Slides App

### In SiteSpeakAI

Log into your SiteSpeakAI account and select your agent from the dropdown menu at the top, click on the **Install Agent** section in the sidebar, select the **Javascript** tab and then click on the **Copy Code** button to copy your agent code snippet.

![Copy code snippet](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/iDv27OshFqOfvRyCYqWLXxYSWUUqYTHq1K26523I.png)

### In Designmodo

1. Log into your **Designmodo** account and select **slides** -> **Open App** on the **Apps** dropdown

![Open Slides app](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/oqmpkP9OwNMKv4oy8V70RRFzo0bNoKc3BwUoieE4.png)

2. For the website you want to add your AI support agent to, click on the hamburger menu and select **Edit**

![Edit website](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/ClB9XqWjM87p4hlbKUn1TnjgbF8aIyEWyoU8uUL2.png)

3. Select **Settings** on the left sidebar and select the **Custom Code** tab

![Custom Code tab](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/DOylzdpxeGICJWqe2vwUCjtX4FWdm7UultKTw10x.png)

4. Under **Custom Code** insert your AI support agent snippet in anyone of the available **Injection** areas, i.e. **Head Code Injection**, **Body Start Code Injection** or **Body End Code Injection**

![Insert code](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/G78gBWhY1paGMQHc3CnMCPwUF961mpli6Jp4Ph8l.png)

5. Click on **Save Changes** and **Publish**

![Save and publish](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/n8KVgkNsLAdgWdhroqgONqKT8h9VjQ3gugx8uy7B.png)

6. Your AI support agent button will now be displayed on your **Designmodo slides** website page

![Agent button displayed](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/3YCBZ3sCJ4dLMLoKZrNel5NpvrO7SeL4B1AnruNL.png)

7. If you click on the button it will display the AI support agent dialogue

![Agent dialogue](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/lPoawUk4TzBcrStqbaig32PAoTYD6EE5ktb0Yavg.png)

Your AI support agent has now been added to your **Designmodo slides** website and your visitors/customers can start interacting with it!

## B. Add your AI support agent to your Designmodo website using the Startup App

### In SiteSpeakAI

Log into your SiteSpeakAI account and select your agent from the dropdown menu at the top, click on the **Install Agent** section in the sidebar, select the **Javascript** tab and then click on the **Copy Code** button to copy your agent code snippet.

![Copy code snippet](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/sn6v3HUhLY90Q2KLalRzqjfa5Ldw5SkwJJGLxWXi.png)

### In Designmodo

1. Log into your **Designmodo** account and select **startup** -> **Open App** on the **Apps** dropdown

![Open Startup app](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/3xCt1GAf0CwAar8ShQskYF52vMfIXwiLVyuYP64M.png)

2. Make sure your project is selected and on the right sidebar, scroll down to **Code injections**

![Code injections](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/ceqaURbS0rwJYig4K3UoTpNQH0vBNqWwjZ7OoOd9.png)

3. Under **Code injections** insert your AI support agent snippet in anyone of the available **Injection** areas, i.e. **Head**, **Body start** or **Body end** and click on **Save** (it does an Autosave)

![Insert and save](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/MJbiaFcnpBPMMhxKDpS5MbCtQKKgIwaqP7v1X9DB.png)

4. Your AI support agent button will now be displayed on your **Designmodo startup** website page

![Agent button displayed](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/GgEKrtsew8JwVFqLc5VcNnPpSYSyMMEoYE7QiGys.png)

5. If you click on the button it will display the AI support agent dialogue

![Agent dialogue](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/DbcdxUtWpfgzxUV8pfVO6EutqL9NeJpVto8Q9aBU.png)

Your AI support agent has now been added to your **Designmodo startup** website and your visitors/customers can start interacting with it!
