You want to embed your custom trained AI support agent in your **Netjet** website page. Here's how to add an AI support agent to Netjet with SiteSpeakAI.

**Netjet** is a website builder designed to be incredibly simple and fast to use, especially for small businesses and individuals who don't have any technical skills. Think of it like a drag-and-drop tool that lets you build a basic website without coding.

**Website URL:** [https://netjet.io/](https://netjet.io/)

## Add your AI support agent to your **Netjet** website

### In SiteSpeakAI

Log into your SiteSpeakAI account and select your agent from the dropdown menu at the top, click on the **Install Agent** section in the sidebar, select the **Javascript** tab and then click on the **Copy Code** button to copy your agent code snippet.
![uploads/help-center/images/kmHu43CeOxYrWp0No1WdZalcqOvsLlswomU2rNLR.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/kmHu43CeOxYrWp0No1WdZalcqOvsLlswomU2rNLR.png)

### In Netjet

1. Log in to your **Netjet** website builder dashboard and select your project
![uploads/help-center/images/N6kqq7FVx1JyGGbMa8bTX0z6oKdBUq6KxKBuzx1U.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/N6kqq7FVx1JyGGbMa8bTX0z6oKdBUq6KxKBuzx1U.png)

2. Click on the **CMS** hamburger menu item on the left-side toolbar
![uploads/help-center/images/S8A1pO8LwkhoAUlx5EFKQ7FC5tBGGoLsGwPsF5Kw.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/S8A1pO8LwkhoAUlx5EFKQ7FC5tBGGoLsGwPsF5Kw.png)

3. Click on **Project Settings**
![uploads/help-center/images/FTSh6nV1yBNXkPqeyyTtTMDTLVyQ4miJarPv6bvS.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/FTSh6nV1yBNXkPqeyyTtTMDTLVyQ4miJarPv6bvS.png)

4. Select the **CODE** tab
![uploads/help-center/images/Zyzi09CUGDPH3YatiIsIWSkDzvZpVq04sGzW02FI.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/Zyzi09CUGDPH3YatiIsIWSkDzvZpVq04sGzW02FI.png)

5. Insert your snippet in the **Code injection > Header** text input box and click on **Save Changes** to update your website
![uploads/help-center/images/msGIsgkqGIKClb3yLna7F92WccBhlxLYDFXhp0Lq.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/msGIsgkqGIKClb3yLna7F92WccBhlxLYDFXhp0Lq.png)

6. Your AI support agent button will now be displayed on your **Netjet** website page
![uploads/help-center/images/Bri7j7kOxBjhtXlU2aIPaZzkxTY5u8ajKv70H8fY.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/Bri7j7kOxBjhtXlU2aIPaZzkxTY5u8ajKv70H8fY.png)

7. If you click on the button it will display the AI support agent dialogue
![uploads/help-center/images/TXa5Fnk3mEfXQDoXBdcKhckoxobMgXThBkOMguZB.png](https://nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/cdn.sitespeak.ai/uploads/help-center/images/TXa5Fnk3mEfXQDoXBdcKhckoxobMgXThBkOMguZB.png)

Your AI support agent has now been added to your **Netjet** website and your visitors/customers can start interacting with it!
